# LightningComponent Documentation for AI-Assisted Modding

This documentation details the `LightningComponent` in Noita, a crucial element for understanding and modifying how lightning effects function within the game. For AI-assisted modding, grasping the parameters and behaviors of this component is essential for creating custom lightning spells, environmental effects, or altering existing electrical phenomena.

## Understanding LightningComponent

The `LightningComponent` is attached to entities that produce or are affected by lightning. It governs various aspects of lightning generation, including its visual appearance, damage, and behavior.
