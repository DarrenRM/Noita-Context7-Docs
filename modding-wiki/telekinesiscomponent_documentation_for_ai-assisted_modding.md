# TelekinesisComponent Documentation for AI-Assisted Modding

This document details the `TelekinesisComponent` in Noita, a crucial component for understanding and implementing telekinetic abilities in your mods. By understanding its properties and how it functions, you can create more dynamic and interactive gameplay elements, such as custom spells or enemy behaviors that manipulate objects.

## Understanding TelekinesisComponent

The `TelekinesisComponent` is responsible for the telekinetic properties of an entity. This includes its ability to be picked up, thrown, and its general interaction with telekinetic forces.
