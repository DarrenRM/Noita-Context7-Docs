# ItemRechargeNearGroundComponent

This documentation covers the `ItemRechargeNearGroundComponent` in Noita, a component that allows items to recharge when the player is near the ground. Understanding this component is crucial for modders who wish to create items with unique recharge mechanics, influencing gameplay by providing buffs or enabling special abilities under specific conditions.

## ItemRechargeNearGroundComponent

The `ItemRechargeNearGroundComponent` is a component that can be added to items to grant them a recharge effect when the player is close to the ground. This can be used to create items that provide benefits, such as increased spell damage or faster cooldowns, when the player is in a grounded state.
