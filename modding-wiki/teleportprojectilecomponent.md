# TeleportProjectileComponent

This documentation covers the `TeleportProjectileComponent` in Noita, a crucial component for creating projectiles that can teleport. Understanding its properties and how to configure them is essential for modders looking to implement unique projectile behaviors, such as instant travel, phased attacks, or projectiles that bypass obstacles.

## Overview

The `TeleportProjectileComponent` allows a projectile to instantly move from its current position to a target position. This component is highly configurable, enabling a wide range of teleportation effects.
