# EnergyShieldComponent Documentation for AI-Assisted Modding

This document details the `EnergyShieldComponent` in Noita, a crucial component for creating and modifying energy shield mechanics. Understanding its properties and how to implement it is essential for modders looking to add new shield types, alter existing ones, or integrate shield functionality into custom items and spells.

## EnergyShieldComponent

The `EnergyShieldComponent` is responsible for managing the behavior and properties of energy shields in Noita. This includes their capacity, regeneration, visual effects, and how they interact with damage.
