# PhysicsThrowableComponent

This documentation covers the `PhysicsThrowableComponent` in Noita, a crucial component for defining how objects behave when thrown. Understanding and modifying this component is essential for creating custom projectiles, grenades, and other throwable items with unique physics interactions in your Noita mods.

## Overview

The `PhysicsThrowableComponent` dictates the physics properties and behaviors of entities that can be thrown. This includes aspects like their initial velocity, how they interact with gravity, their collision properties, and any special effects that occur upon impact or when thrown.

## Component Properties

The `PhysicsThrowableComponent` is defined within the entity's XML file. Here are the key properties you can modify:

### `throwable_component`

This is the main tag for the component.
