# ProjectileComponent Documentation

This documentation details the `ProjectileComponent` in Noita, a crucial component for defining the behavior of projectiles. Understanding and modifying this component is essential for creating custom spells, weapons, and projectile effects in your Noita mods.

## ProjectileComponent

The `ProjectileComponent` is attached to entities that function as projectiles. It governs their movement, collision, damage, and various other projectile-specific properties.
