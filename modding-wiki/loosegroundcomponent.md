# LooseGroundComponent

This documentation page details the `LooseGroundComponent` in Noita, a crucial component for defining how ground tiles behave, particularly their interaction with physics and destruction. Understanding this component is essential for modders looking to customize terrain, create new destructible elements, or alter the physical properties of the ground in their mods.

## Overview of LooseGroundComponent

The `LooseGroundComponent` is attached to entities that represent ground tiles or similar destructible terrain elements. It governs aspects like how much damage the ground can take before breaking, what materials it breaks into, and its general physical properties.

## Component Properties

The `LooseGroundComponent` has several properties that can be configured within an entity's XML definition. These properties allow for fine-grained control over the ground's behavior.
