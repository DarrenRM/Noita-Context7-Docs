# FossilComponent Documentation

This document details the `FossilComponent` in Noita, a crucial component for defining and managing fossilized entities within the game. Understanding `FossilComponent` is essential for modders looking to create new fossil types, modify existing ones, or integrate fossil mechanics into their mods.

## FossilComponent Properties

The `FossilComponent` is attached to entities that represent fossils. It governs various aspects of their behavior and appearance.
