# AudioComponent Documentation

This documentation provides a comprehensive guide to the `AudioComponent` in Noita, detailing its properties, functionalities, and how to utilize it within your mods. Understanding `AudioComponent` is crucial for adding custom sound effects, music, and ambient audio to your Noita creations, enhancing the player experience and immersion.

## Overview of AudioComponent

The `AudioComponent` is a fundamental entity in Noita responsible for managing and playing sound effects and music. It allows modders to trigger audio events based on various game conditions, character actions, or environmental changes. This component is essential for bringing your mods to life with dynamic and engaging audio.

## Core Concepts and Properties

The `AudioComponent` is configured within `.xml` files, typically associated with entities. Its behavior is defined by a set of properties that control playback, looping, volume, pitch, and more.
