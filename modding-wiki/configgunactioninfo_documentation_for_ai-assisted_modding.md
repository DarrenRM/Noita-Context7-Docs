# ConfigGunActionInfo Documentation for AI-Assisted Modding

This documentation provides a detailed breakdown of the `ConfigGunActionInfo` component in Noita, crucial for understanding and modifying how guns behave. By leveraging this information, AI-assisted modding tools can more effectively generate, analyze, and suggest changes to gun configurations, enabling complex custom weapon behaviors.

## Understanding ConfigGunActionInfo

The `ConfigGunActionInfo` component is central to defining the actions a gun can perform. It dictates what happens when a gun is fired, including the projectiles it spawns, their properties, and any associated effects. This section details the various parameters and their impact on gun behavior.
