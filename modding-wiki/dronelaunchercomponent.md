# DroneLauncherComponent

This documentation covers the `DroneLauncherComponent` in Noita, a crucial component for creating entities that can spawn and manage drones. Understanding this component is essential for modders who wish to implement custom drone-launching mechanics, such as new weapons, enemy behaviors, or utility items.

## Overview

The `DroneLauncherComponent` is attached to an entity and dictates its behavior as a drone launcher. It defines what kind of drones are launched, how they are launched, and their associated properties. This component is primarily configured within the entity's XML definition.
