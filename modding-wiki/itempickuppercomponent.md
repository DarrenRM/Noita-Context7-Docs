# ItemPickUpperComponent

This documentation page details the `ItemPickUpperComponent` in Noita, a crucial component for understanding how items are picked up by entities. For modders, grasping its functionality is essential for creating custom items, modifying pickup behavior, or implementing unique item interactions within your mods.

## Overview

The `ItemPickUpperComponent` is attached to entities that are capable of picking up items. It defines the parameters and logic that govern how an entity interacts with and collects other entities designated as items. This includes aspects like the range at which items can be picked up, the types of items that can be collected, and any associated effects or conditions.
