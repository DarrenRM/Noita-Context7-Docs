# ParticleEmitterComponent Documentation

This document details the `ParticleEmitterComponent` in Noita, a crucial component for creating and managing visual effects like particles. Understanding its properties and how to configure them is essential for modders looking to add dynamic visual flair to their creations, from spell effects to environmental details.

## Overview of ParticleEmitterComponent

The `ParticleEmitterComponent` is responsible for generating and controlling particle systems within Noita. These systems can range from simple smoke puffs to complex magical effects. By modifying this component, modders can influence the appearance, behavior, and lifespan of emitted particles, enabling a wide array of visual customization.

## Component Properties

The `ParticleEmitterComponent` has numerous properties that control various aspects of particle emission. These are typically configured within an entity's XML definition.
