# PhysicsBodyCollisionDamageComponent

This documentation page details the `PhysicsBodyCollisionDamageComponent` in Noita, a crucial component for defining how entities inflict damage upon colliding with other physics bodies. Understanding and modifying this component is essential for creating custom damage mechanics, balancing combat, or implementing unique environmental hazards in your Noita mods.

## Overview

The `PhysicsBodyCollisionDamageComponent` is attached to entities that should deal damage when they collide with other objects in the game world. It allows for fine-grained control over the damage dealt, including the base damage amount, how it scales with velocity, and the types of entities it can affect.
