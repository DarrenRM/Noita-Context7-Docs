# AIComponent Documentation for Modding

This document provides a comprehensive guide to the `AIComponent` in Noita, a crucial element for understanding and modifying enemy and NPC behaviors. It details the various parameters and functionalities of the `AIComponent`, enabling modders to create custom AI routines, alter existing behaviors, and integrate new entities with unique artificial intelligence.

## Understanding the AIComponent

The `AIComponent` is a fundamental part of Noita's entity system, responsible for defining how non-player characters (NPCs) and enemies perceive their environment, make decisions, and execute actions. By modifying or creating new `AIComponent` configurations, modders can significantly impact gameplay by introducing new enemy types, altering the difficulty, or creating unique interactive elements.

### Core Functionality

The `AIComponent` dictates a wide range of behaviors, including:

*   **Perception:** How an entity detects players, other entities, and environmental hazards.
*   **Decision Making:** The logic behind an entity's choices, such as attacking, fleeing, or patrolling.
*   **Movement:** How an entity navigates the game world.
*   **Action Execution:** The specific actions an entity performs, like casting spells or using items.
