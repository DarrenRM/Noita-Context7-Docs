# SpriteParticleEmitterComponent

This documentation page details the `SpriteParticleEmitterComponent` in Noita, a crucial component for creating and managing particle effects that are tied to sprites. Understanding this component is essential for modders looking to add dynamic visual flair to their creations, from custom spell effects to environmental details.

## Overview

The `SpriteParticleEmitterComponent` allows you to define and control particle emitters that are attached to a sprite. This means particles will originate from and move with the sprite they are associated with, enabling a wide range of visual effects. This component is configured within the entity's XML definition.

The `SpriteParticleEmitterComponent` has several properties that can be configured to customize the behavior of the particle emitter.
