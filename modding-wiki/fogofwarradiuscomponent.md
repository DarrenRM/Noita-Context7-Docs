# FogOfWarRadiusComponent

This documentation page details the `FogOfWarRadiusComponent` in Noita, a crucial component for managing the fog of war mechanics. Understanding this component is essential for modders who wish to alter or create custom entities that interact with or are affected by the game's visibility system.

## FogOfWarRadiusComponent

The `FogOfWarRadiusComponent` is responsible for defining the radius around an entity that affects the fog of war. This means it controls how much of the map is revealed or obscured based on the entity's presence.
