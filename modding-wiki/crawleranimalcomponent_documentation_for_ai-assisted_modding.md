# CrawlerAnimalComponent Documentation for AI-Assisted Modding

This documentation covers the `CrawlerAnimalComponent` in Noita, a crucial component for defining the behavior of crawling enemies. Understanding this component is essential for modders looking to create or modify enemy AI, influencing their movement, targeting, and interaction with the environment.

## CrawlerAnimalComponent

The `CrawlerAnimalComponent` is responsible for the AI logic of enemies that crawl. This includes their movement patterns, how they navigate terrain, and their general behavior in the game world.
