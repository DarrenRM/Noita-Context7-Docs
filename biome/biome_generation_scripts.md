# Biome Generation Scripts

This document outlines the key biome generation scripts called by `__parse_all.bat`. These scripts define the structure and elements of various biomes within Noita.

## Core Biome Generation Scripts

The following scripts are responsible for generating distinct biomes. Each script typically defines parameters for terrain, enemy spawns, and environmental features.

### `_boss_arena.bat`

*   **Purpose:** Generates the Boss Arena biome.
*   **Key Elements:**
    *   Unique terrain generation for a boss encounter.
    *   Specific enemy configurations for boss fights.
    *   Potentially unique environmental hazards or interactive elements.

### `_giant_tree.bat`

*   **Purpose:** Generates the Giant Tree biome.
*   **Key Elements:**
    *   Dominant vertical terrain structure.
    *   Unique flora and fauna associated with a large tree.
    *   Potential for platforming challenges.

### `_lake_statue.bat`

*   **Purpose:** Generates a biome featuring a lake and a statue.
*   **Key Elements:**
    *   Aquatic terrain elements.
    *   A prominent statue as a landmark.
    *   Enemies and hazards related to water environments.

### `_lavalake2.bat`

*   **Purpose:** Generates a lava lake biome (likely a variation or expansion).
*   **Key Elements:**
    *   Extensive lava pools and flows.
    *   Heat-based hazards and enemies.
    *   Unique rock formations and structures.

### `_lavalake_pit_bottom.bat`

*   **Purpose:** Generates the bottom of a lava lake pit.
*   **Key Elements:**
    *   Deep, hazardous lava environment.
    *   Potentially unique challenges at the lowest points.
    *   Specific enemy types adapted to extreme heat.

### `_moon.bat`

*   **Purpose:** Generates the Moon biome.
*   **Key Elements:**
    *   Low-gravity environment.
    *   Unique lunar terrain and rock formations.
    *   Specific alien enemies and hazards.

### `_mountain_lake.bat`

*   **Purpose:** Generates a mountain lake biome.
*   **Key Elements:**
    *   Rocky, elevated terrain.
    *   Cold water bodies.
    *   Enemies and environmental features typical of mountainous regions.

### `_tree.bat`

*   **Purpose:** Generates a standard tree biome.
*   **Key Elements:**
    *   Forest-like terrain.
    *   Various tree types and foliage.
    *   Common forest-dwelling enemies.

### `_watercave.bat`

*   **Purpose:** Generates a water cave biome.
*   **Key Elements:**
    *   Subterranean environments with water.
    *   Cave formations and structures.
    *   Enemies and hazards found in damp, enclosed spaces.
