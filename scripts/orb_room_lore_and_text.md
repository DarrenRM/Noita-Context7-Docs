# Orb Room Lore and Text

This document compiles lore text and associated elements found in Noita's orb rooms, primarily for AI-assisted modding reference. It highlights key thematic elements and potential triggers for each orb.

## Orb Room Themes and Lore

The lore presented in these orb rooms draws heavily from alchemical texts and philosophical concepts, often referencing the pursuit of perfection, transformation, secrecy, and the nature of existence.

### Orb 7 (Lavalake)

*   **Theme:** The pursuit of perfection in alchemy, the all-or-nothing nature of the Art.
*   **Key Text Snippet:** "A person who studies this Art, must have either everything or nothing."
*   **Associated Element:** `/Thunder cloud`

### Orb 3 (Below Lavalake)

*   **Theme:** Warning and consequence, technological advancement.
*   **Key Text Snippet:** "Head my warning or death and decay will follow."
*   **Associated Element:** `/Nuke`

### Orb 0 (On Mountain)

*   **Theme:** Hermetic principles, the interconnectedness of the universe.
*   **Key Text Snippet:** "That which is below is like that which is above and that which is above is like that is below to do the miracles of one only thing."
*   **Associated Element:** `/Sea of Lava`

### Orb 1 (Above Pyramid)

*   **Theme:** Secrecy and oaths within the alchemical community.
*   **Key Text Snippet:** "We are sworn to secrecy by heaven and earth and hell... to reveal nothing clearly to any uninitiated being."
*   **Associated Element:** `/earth quake`

### Orb 4 (Below Pyramid)

*   **Theme:** The nature of life, death, and the role of "fire" as a vital spirit.
*   **Key Text Snippet:** "The definition of death is the disjunction of the composite... anything formed out of two, three, or four components must disintegrate, and this is death."
*   **Associated Element:** `/Necromancy`

### Orb 10 (Below Desert)

*   **Theme:** Alchemical processes, specifically the transformation of materials through heat and liquefaction.
*   **Key Text Snippet:** "Cook the copper until it become a gentle and impalpable body... until it again becomes dry, and is made earth."
*   **Associated Element:** `/cement`
*   **Coordinates:** `10479, 16048`

### Orb 8 (Hell)

*   **Theme:** Ignorance of the Art, the transformative power of nature, and its mastery.
*   **Key Text Snippet:** "Woe unto all ye fools! How ignorant are ye of this Art... O how this nature changeth body into spirit!"
*   **Associated Element:** `/fireworks`
*   **Potential Lore:** Mad God of Knowledge?

### Orb 9 (Below Lumen)

*   **Theme:** Nature's generative processes, specifically in the formation of metals.
*   **Key Text Snippet:** "In this way, then, Nature generates by means of this sulphur the different kinds of metals in the veins of the earth."
*   **Associated Element:** `/exploding deer`
*   **Status:** WIP

### Orb 06 (Vault)

*   **Theme:** The importance of diligent work and perseverance in alchemy, contrasting with idleness.
*   **Key Text Snippet:** "Some Alchemists fancy that the work from beginning to end is a mere idle entertainment; but those who make it so will reap what they have sown -- nothing."
*   **Associated Element:** `/spiral shot`

### Orb 05 (Wand Cave)

*   **Theme:** A humorous, biblical-style instruction for using a specific item.
*   **Key Text Snippet:** "'First shalt thou take out the Holy Pin. Then, shalt thou count to three... lobbest thou thy Holy Hand Grenade of Antioch towards thy foe.'"
*   **Associated Element:** `/Holy hand grenade`

### Orb 02 (Snow Vault)

*   **Theme:** The creation of primal spirits, often associated with duality.
*   **Key Text Snippet:** "'thence is born a spirit white as snow, and another spirit red as blood, which two spirits are contained in a third hidden thing.'"
*   **Associated Element:** `/tentacle`

## General Alchemical Concepts and References

The text also contains broader alchemical and philosophical musings that could inform modding:

*   **The Great Work:** The ultimate goal of alchemy, often described as achieving perfection, immortality, or transmutation.
*   **Secrecy:** A recurring theme, emphasizing the need to conceal alchemical knowledge from the uninitiated.
*   **The Nature of Elements:** Discussions on "water" that is not literal water, but a fundamental, permanent substance.
*   **Transformation:** The core concept of changing one substance into another, or one state into another.
*   **Perseverance and Industry:** The necessity of hard work and dedication in the pursuit of alchemical goals.

### Notable Quotes and Concepts

*   "It is neither religious nor wise to judge that of which you know nothing."
*   "The necessity of concealing the Art is one of the chief anxieties of alchemists."
*   "The definition of death is the disjunction of the composite, but there is no disjunction of that which is simple, for it is one."
*   "Tis true without lying, certain and most true. That which is below is like that which is above and that which is above is like that is below to do the miracles of one only thing."
*   Discussions on "permanent water" and its role in alchemical processes.
*   The idea that true wealth lies not in material riches but in the "Medicine" that grants continued life and health.
*   The contrast between many students and few masters in any given art.
