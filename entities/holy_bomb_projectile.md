# Holy Bomb Projectile

This document details the configuration of the Holy Bomb projectile entity in Noita, focusing on its key attributes for AI-assisted modding.

## Core Projectile Properties

The `ProjectileComponent` defines the fundamental behavior and effects of the projectile.

### Key Attributes:

*   **`lifetime`**: `170` - The duration in frames before the projectile self-destructs.
*   **`damage`**: `0` - The base damage dealt by the projectile itself (before explosion).
*   **`muzzle_flash_file`**: `data/entities/particles/muzzle_flashes/muzzle_flash_magic_launcher_holy.xml` - Specifies the particle effect used for the muzzle flash when fired.
*   **`shoot_light_flash_radius`**: `120` - The radius of the light flash emitted upon shooting.
*   **`shoot_light_flash_r`, `shoot_light_flash_g`, `shoot_light_flash_b`**: `255`, `240`, `30` - RGB values for the shooting light flash color (a bright yellow).

### Explosion Configuration (`config_explosion`)

This nested element defines the properties of the explosion that occurs when the projectile hits a surface or its lifetime ends.

*   **`damage`**: `8` - The damage dealt by the explosion.
*   **`camera_shake`**: `60` - The intensity of camera shake upon explosion.
*   **`explosion_radius`**: `180` - The radius of the explosion's effect.
*   **`explosion_sprite`**: `data/particles/explosion_128.xml` - The sprite used for the main explosion visual.
*   **`load_this_entity`**: `data/entities/particles/particle_explosion/main_large_radius.xml,data/entities/misc/loose_ground.xml,data/entities/misc/explosion_was_here.xml` - Entities to load and spawn upon explosion, contributing to the visual and physical effects.
*   **`create_cell_probability`**: `40` - The chance (in percent) of creating liquid cells upon explosion.
*   **`hole_enabled`**: `1` - Enables the creation of holes in terrain.
*   **`ray_energy`**: `6400000` - The energy value associated with the explosion's destructive rays.
*   **`physics_explosion_power.min`**: `4.5` - Minimum force applied to physics objects in the explosion.
*   **`physics_explosion_power.max`**: `9` - Maximum force applied to physics objects in the explosion.
*   **`sparks_count_max`, `sparks_count_min`**: `1500`, `1600` - The range for the number of sparks generated by the explosion.
*   **`stains_radius`**: `35` - The radius of stains left by the explosion.
*   **`background_lightning_count`**: `5` - The number of background lightning effects to spawn.
*   **`audio_event_name`**: `explosions/holy` - The sound event triggered by the explosion.

## Visual and Audio Components

### Graphics (`PhysicsImageShapeComponent`)

*   **`image_file`**: `data/items_gfx/bomb_holy.png` - The sprite used for the projectile's visual representation.
*   **`material`**: `fuse_holy` - The material type, likely influencing its interaction with other game elements.

### Particle Emitters

The projectile utilizes several `ParticleEmitterComponent`s to generate visual effects.

*   **Smoke Emitter**:
    *   `emitted_material_name`: `smoke`
    *   Generates small amounts of smoke with low lifetime.
*   **Spark Emitter (Primary)**:
    *   `emitted_material_name`: `spark`
    *   Generates a moderate number of cosmetic sparks with varying lifetimes and some airflow influence.
*   **Spark Emitter (Sparse Trail)**:
    *   `emitted_material_name`: `spark`
    *   Generates very few sparks with longer lifetimes, creating a sparse trail effect with airflow.
