# Flamethrower Projectile

This document details the configuration of the Flamethrower projectile entity in Noita, focusing on its core attributes and behaviors for AI-assisted modding.

## Core Entity Properties

*   **`name`**: `$projectile_default` - A generic name, often overridden by specific projectile types.
*   **`tags`**: `projectile_player` - Identifies this entity as a projectile fired by the player.

## Base Projectile Configuration (`BaseComponent`)

This section inherits properties from the base projectile entity.

### Velocity Component

*   **`gravity_y`**: `100` - The gravitational pull affecting the projectile vertically.
*   **`mass`**: `0.067` - The mass of the projectile, influencing its interaction with physics.

## Projectile Component (`ProjectileComponent`)

This is the primary component defining the projectile's behavior.

### Key Attributes

*   **`lob_min` / `lob_max`**: `0.8` / `1.0` - Controls the minimum and maximum lobbing angle of the projectile.
*   **`speed_min` / `speed_max`**: `160` / `170` - Defines the projectile's initial speed range.
*   **`die_on_low_velocity`**: `1` - The projectile will be destroyed if its velocity drops too low.
*   **`on_death_explode`**: `1` - The projectile explodes upon death.
*   **`on_lifetime_out_explode`**: `1` - The projectile explodes when its lifetime expires.
*   **`explosion_dont_damage_shooter`**: `1` - Prevents the projectile's explosion from damaging the player who fired it.
*   **`damage`**: `0` - Base damage of the projectile itself (before explosion).
*   **`die_on_liquid_collision`**: `1` - The projectile is destroyed upon colliding with liquids.
*   **`on_collision_die`**: `1` - The projectile is destroyed upon any collision.
*   **`lifetime`**: `80` - The duration in frames before the projectile expires.
*   **`knockback_force`**: `0.2` - The force applied to entities when the projectile hits them.
*   **`physics_impulse_coeff`**: `1000` - Coefficient for physics impulse calculations.

### Damage Types

*   **`fire`**: `0.45` - The projectile deals 0.45 damage of type 'fire'.

### Explosion Configuration (`config_explosion`)

Defines the properties of the explosion that occurs upon the projectile's death.

*   **`load_this_entity`**: `data/entities/misc/fire.xml` - The entity to load when the explosion occurs (likely a fire effect).
*   **`never_cache`**: `1` - Ensures this explosion effect is not cached and always reloaded.
*   **`camera_shake`**: `0` - Disables camera shake for this explosion.
*   **`explosion_radius`**: `8` - The radius of the explosion.
*   **`create_cell_probability`**: `100` - Always creates material cells upon explosion.
*   **`create_cell_material`**: `fire` - The material created by the explosion is 'fire'.
*   **`hole_enabled`**: `1` - Creates a hole in the environment where the explosion occurs.
*   **`damage`**: `0.5` - The damage dealt by the explosion.
*   **`particle_effect`**: `1` - Enables particle effects for the explosion.
*   **`damage_mortals`**: `1` - The explosion damages mortal entities.
*   **`physics_explosion_power.min` / `.max`**: `0.6` / `0.9` - The minimum and maximum power of the physics-based explosion force.
*   **`ray_energy`**: `5000` - The energy of any rays emitted by the explosion.
*   **`physics_throw_enabled`**: `1` - Enables physics-based throwing of entities affected by the explosion.
*   **`shake_vegetation`**: `1` - Causes vegetation to shake from the explosion.
*   **`sparks_enabled`**: `1` - Enables sparks to be generated by the explosion.
*   **`stains_enabled`**: `1` - Enables stains to be left by the explosion.

## Sprite Component (`SpriteComponent`)

Defines the visual representation of the projectile.

*   **`image_file`**: `data/projectiles_gfx/fireball_alt.xml` - The sprite file used for the projectile.
*   **`offset_x` / `offset_y`**: `16` / `12` - The offset of the sprite from the projectile's origin.
*   **`additive`**: `1` - Uses additive blending for the sprite, common for fire effects.
