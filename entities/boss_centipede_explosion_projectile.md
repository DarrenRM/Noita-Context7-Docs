# Boss Centipede Explosion Projectile

This entity defines the projectile that explodes from the Boss Centipede. It's a projectile with a short lifespan that detonates on death or when its lifetime expires, creating a significant explosion effect.

## Key Components

### Base Projectile (`<Base file="data/entities/base_projectile.xml">`)

*   **`VelocityComponent`**:
    *   `gravity_y="50"`: Applies a moderate downward gravity to the projectile.
    *   `air_friction="-5.0"`: Reduces the projectile's velocity due to air resistance.
    *   `mass="0.05"`: Defines the projectile's mass.

### Projectile Component (`<ProjectileComponent>`)

This is the core component defining the projectile's behavior.

*   **`_enabled="1"`**: Enables the component.
*   **`lob_min="0.8"`, `lob_max="1.0"`**: Controls the lobbing behavior of the projectile.
*   **`speed_min="1"`, `speed_max="1"`**: Sets the projectile's speed.
*   **`direction_random_rad="0.0"`**: Ensures the projectile travels in a straight line without random deviation.
*   **`on_death_explode="1"`**: Triggers an explosion when the projectile dies.
*   **`on_lifetime_out_explode="1"`**: Triggers an explosion when the projectile's lifetime ends.
*   **`explosion_dont_damage_shooter="1"`**: Prevents the explosion from damaging the entity that fired it.
*   **`on_collision_die="0"`**: The projectile does not die upon collision, only explodes when its conditions are met.
*   **`lifetime="5"`**: The projectile exists for 5 seconds before expiring.
*   **`damage="0"`**: The projectile itself deals no direct damage. Damage is handled by the explosion.
*   **`velocity_sets_scale="1"`**: The projectile's velocity influences its scale.
*   **`lifetime_randomness="0"`**: The projectile's lifetime is fixed.
*   **`ragdoll_force_multiplier="0"`**: No ragdoll force is applied by the projectile itself.
*   **`hit_particle_force_multiplier="0"`**: No particle force is applied on hit.
*   **`camera_shake_when_shot="0"`**: No camera shake when the projectile is fired.
*   **`knockback_force="0.0"`**: No knockback is applied by the projectile.

#### Explosion Configuration (`<config_explosion>`)

This nested element defines the properties of the explosion that occurs.

*   **`never_cache="1"`**: Ensures this explosion effect is not cached, allowing for dynamic creation.
*   **`camera_shake="20"`**: The explosion causes significant camera shake.
*   **`explosion_radius="50"`**: The explosion affects an area with a radius of 50 units.
*   **`load_this_entity="..."`**: Specifies entities to load upon explosion, including a custom explosion effect and blue particles.
    *   `data/entities/animals/boss_centipede/boss_centipede_explosion_effect.xml`
    *   `data/entities/particles/particle_explosion/main_blue.xml`
*   **`create_cell_probability="10"`**: A 10% chance to create cell-like structures from the explosion.
*   **`cell_explosion_power_ragdoll_coeff="0.5"`**: Coefficient for ragdoll force when cells are created.
*   **`hole_enabled="1"`**: Enables the creation of holes in the environment.
*   **`ray_energy="200000"`**: High energy for destruction via raycasting.
*   **`max_durability_to_destroy="15"`**: The maximum durability of objects that can be destroyed by the explosion.
*   **`damage="1.2"`**: The explosion deals 1.2 damage.
*   **`particle_effect="1"`**: Enables particle effects for the explosion.
*   **`damage_mortals="1"`**: The explosion damages living entities.
*   **`physics_explosion_power.min="1.0"`, `physics_explosion_power.max="1.4"`**: Defines the minimum and maximum physics force applied by the explosion.
*   **`physics_throw_enabled="1"`**: Objects affected by the explosion can be thrown.
*   **`shake_vegetation="1"`**: The explosion shakes vegetation.
*   **`spark_material="plasma_fading"`**: The material used for sparks generated by the explosion.
*   **`sparks_count_min="7"`, `sparks_count_max="20"`**: The number of sparks generated.
*   **`stains_enabled="1"`**: Enables stain effects from the explosion.
*   **`stains_radius="15"`**: The radius of the stains created.

### Light Component (`<LightComponent>`)

*   **`r="35"`, `g="210"`, `b="255"`**: Defines the light color as a bright cyan.
*   **`radius="150"`**: The light emitted by the projectile has a radius of 150 units.

### Particle Emitter Components (`<ParticleEmitterComponent>`)

Multiple emitters are used to create a visual effect of plasma particles.

*   **`emitted_material_name="plasma_fading"`**: The material used for the emitted particles.
*   **`count_min`/`count_max`**: Controls the number of particles emitted per burst.
*   **`gravity.y`**: Varies the gravity applied to particles from different emitters (positive, negative, or zero).
*   **`lifetime_min`/`lifetime_max`**: Sets the duration particles exist.
*   **`airflow_force`/`airflow_time`/`airflow_scale`**: Controls how air currents affect particles.
*   **`area_circle_radius.max="35"`**: Particles are emitted within a circle of this radius.
*   **`is_trail="1"`**: Some emitters create trails.
*   **`render_on_grid="1"`**: Particles are rendered on the game grid.
*   **`fade_based_on_lifetime="1"`**: Particles fade out as their lifetime ends.
*   **`emit_cosmetic_particles="1"`**: Emits cosmetic particles for visual flair.
*   **`emission_interval_min_frames`/`emission_interval_max_frames`**: Controls the timing of particle emissions.
*   **Sparse Emitter**: One emitter is configured for less frequent, more directed particle bursts.

### Audio Component (`<AudioComponent>`)

*   **`file="data/audio/Desktop/projectiles.bank"`**: Specifies the audio bank containing projectile sounds.
*   **`event_root="player_projectiles/bullet_rocket"`**: Sets the root event for projectile sounds.

### Variable Storage Component (`<VariableStorageComponent>`)

*   **`name="projectile_file"`**: Stores the filename of this entity.
*   **`value_string="data/entities/animals/boss_centipede/boss_centipede_explosion.xml"`**: The value is the path to this entity's XML file.
