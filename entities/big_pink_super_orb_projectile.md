# Big Pink Super Orb Projectile

This document details the configuration of the "Big Pink Super Orb" projectile entity in Noita, focusing on its key attributes for AI-assisted modding.

## Core Entity Configuration

The projectile is defined by its `name` and `tags`.

*   **`name`**: `$projectile_default` - Indicates it inherits default projectile properties.
*   **`tags`**: `resist_repulsion` - This projectile is resistant to repulsion forces.

## Base Projectile Properties

Inherits from `data/entities/base_projectile.xml`.

### `BaseComponent`

*   **`VelocityComponent`**:
    *   `gravity_y="0"`: No vertical gravity applied to the projectile itself.
    *   `air_friction="0"`: No air friction.
    *   `mass="0.8"`: The projectile has a mass of 0.8.

## Projectile Behavior (`ProjectileComponent`)

This component governs the projectile's movement, damage, and death behaviors.

*   **`_enabled="1"`**: The component is active.
*   **`lob_min="0.8"`, `lob_max="1.0"`**: Controls the lobbing behavior, influencing its arc.
*   **`speed_min="60"`, `speed_max="120"`**: The projectile's speed can range from 60 to 120 units.
*   **`direction_nonrandom_rad="1.4"`**: A specific initial direction is set, reducing randomness.
*   **`die_on_low_velocity="0"`**: The projectile does not die if its velocity drops too low.
*   **`on_death_explode="1"`**: The projectile explodes upon death.
*   **`on_lifetime_out_explode="1"`**: The projectile explodes when its lifetime expires.
*   **`explosion_dont_damage_shooter="1"`**: The explosion does not damage the entity that fired it.
*   **`damage="1.0"`**: Base damage dealt by the projectile.
*   **`on_collision_die="1"`**: The projectile dies upon colliding with something.
*   **`lifetime="100"`**: The projectile's base lifetime in frames.
*   **`lifetime_randomness="40"`**: Adds randomness to the lifetime.
*   **`knockback_force="2.0"`**: The force applied to knock back entities upon collision.

### `config_explosion`

Details the explosion effect when the projectile dies.

*   **`never_cache="1"`**: Prevents caching of this explosion.
*   **`camera_shake="35.0"`**: The intensity of camera shake during the explosion.
*   **`explosion_radius="38"`**: The radius of the explosion.
*   **`explosion_sprite="data/particles/explosion_032_pink.xml"`**: The sprite used for the explosion.
*   **`load_this_entity="data/entities/particles/particle_explosion/main_swirly_pink.xml"`**: The entity to load for the explosion effect.
*   **`damage="1.5"`**: Damage dealt by the explosion.
*   **`hole_enabled="1"`**: Creates a hole in the terrain upon explosion.
*   **`hole_destroy_liquid="1"`**: The hole will destroy liquids.
*   **`ray_energy="100000"`**: High energy for potential ray interactions.
*   **`physics_explosion_power.min="0.4"`, `physics_explosion_power.max="0.6"`**: Controls the physics force of the explosion.
*   **`physics_throw_enabled="1"`**: Enables physics-based throwing of objects by the explosion.
*   **`sparks_enabled="1"`**: Sparks are generated by the explosion.
*   **`sparks_count_min="60"`, `sparks_count_max="80"`**: The number of sparks generated.
*   **`spark_material="plasma_fading_pink"`**: The material of the sparks.
*   **`light_r="155"`, `light_g="15"`, `light_b="140"`**: RGB values for the explosion's light.
*   **`stains_enabled="1"`**: Creates stains on surfaces from the explosion.
*   **`audio_event_name="explosions/magic_rocket_mid"`**: The sound event triggered by the explosion.

## Homing Behavior (`HomingComponent`)

This projectile exhibits homing capabilities.

*   **`target_tag="prey"`**: It targets entities with the "prey" tag.
*   **`homing_targeting_coeff="8"`**: The strength of the homing targeting.
*   **`detect_distance="350"`**: The distance at which it can detect targets.
*   **`homing_velocity_multiplier="1.0"`**: Multiplier for velocity when homing.

## Visuals (`SpriteComponent`)

Defines the projectile's appearance.

*   **`image_file="data/projectiles_gfx/orb_pink_big.xml"`**: The primary sprite file.
*   **`rect_animation="fireball"`**: Specifies a "fireball" animation.
*   **`offset_x="8"`, `offset_y="8"`**: Offsets for sprite positioning.
*   **`additive="1"`**: Uses additive blending for the sprite.

## Particle Emitters

Multiple particle emitters contribute to the visual effects.

### `SpriteParticleEmitterComponent` (x2)

These components emit sprites for visual flair.

*   **`sprite_file`**: Specifies the sprite to emit (e.g., `data/particles/orb_pink_big_out.xml` or `data/projectiles_gfx/orb_pink_big.xml`).
*   **`additive="1"`**: Uses additive blending.
*   **`gravity.y="10"`**: Particles are affected by gravity.
*   **`scale_velocity.x/y`**: Controls how the scale of emitted particles changes over time.
*   **`randomize_rotation`**, **`randomize_angular_velocity`**: Adds variation to emitted particle rotation.

### `ParticleEmitterComponent`

Emits particles with specific material properties.

*   **`emitted_material_name="plasma_fading_pink"`**: The material of the emitted particles.
*   **`count_min="1"`, `count_max="16"`**: The number of particles emitted.
*   **`lifetime_min="0.5"`, `lifetime_max="2"`**: The lifetime of emitted particles.
*   **`area_circle_radius.min/max`**: Defines the emission area as a circle.
*   **`velocity_always_away_from_center="20"`**: Particles are pushed away from the center of emission.
*   **`emission_interval_min_frames="1"`, `emission_interval_max_frames="6"`**: Controls the timing of particle emissions.

*   **`r="150"`, `g="35"`, `b="120"`**: The RGB color of the light.

*   **`script_source_file="data/scripts/projectiles/orb_pink_big_super.lua"`**: The path to the Lua script.
*   **`execute_on_removed="1"`**: The script executes when the entity is removed.

## Variable Storage (`VariableStorageComponent`)

Stores custom variables.

*   **`name="projectile_file"`**: The name of the variable.
*   **`value_string="data/entities/projectiles/orb_pink_big_super.xml"`**: The value, storing the projectile's own file path.
