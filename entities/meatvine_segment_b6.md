This component handles the visual representation of the meatvine segment.

*   **`image_file`**: `data/entities/verlet_chains/meatvine/vine_parts/vine_b_6.xml`

    *   Horizontal offset for the sprite.
*   **`offset_y`**: `1.5`
    *   Vertical offset for the sprite.
*   **`update_transform`**: `0`

*   **`update_transform_rotation`**: `0`
