# Power Digger Projectile

This document describes the `powerdigger.xml` entity, which defines the behavior of the Power Digger projectile in Noita. This projectile is designed to break through drillable terrain and leave holes.

## Key Components and Attributes

The Power Digger projectile is primarily defined by its `ProjectileComponent` and `ConfigExplosion` elements.
