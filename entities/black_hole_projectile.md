# Black Hole Projectile

This document details the configuration of the Black Hole projectile in Noita, focusing on its key attributes for AI-assisted modding.

## Entity Definition

The core entity definition for the Black Hole projectile.

```xml
<Entity name="$projectile_default" tags="projectile_player,black_hole">
```

### Key Attributes:

*   **`name`**: `$projectile_default` - A placeholder name, often overridden by specific projectile types.
*   **`tags`**: `projectile_player,black_hole` - Identifies this entity as a player projectile and specifically as a black hole.

## Base Projectile Configuration

Inherits fundamental projectile properties.

```xml
<Base file="data/entities/base_projectile.xml">
	<VelocityComponent
		air_friction="-0.2"
		gravity_y="0"
		mass="0.3"
		>
	</VelocityComponent>
</Base>
```

### Key Attributes:

*   **`VelocityComponent`**:
    *   `air_friction`: `-0.2` - Controls how quickly the projectile loses speed in the air.
    *   `gravity_y`: `0` - The projectile is not affected by gravity.
    *   `mass`: `0.3` - The projectile's mass, influencing its interaction with forces.

### Key Attributes:

*   **`lob_min`, `lob_max`**: `0.8`, `1.0` - Controls the arc of the projectile.
*   **`speed_min`, `speed_max`**: `40` - The projectile travels at a fixed speed.
*   **`collide_with_world`**: `0` - The projectile does not collide with the environment.
*   **`on_death_explode`**: `1` - The projectile explodes upon death.
*   **`on_lifetime_out_explode`**: `1` - The projectile explodes when its lifetime expires.
*   **`lifetime`**: `120` - The duration in frames before the projectile expires.
*   **`damage`**: `0` - The projectile itself deals no direct damage.
*   **`knockback_force`**: `0.1` - The force applied to entities it hits.
*   **`penetrate_entities`**: `1` - The projectile can pass through multiple entities.
*   **`camera_shake_when_shot`**: `0.4` - Triggers a small camera shake when fired.
*   **`muzzle_flash_file`**: `data/entities/particles/muzzle_flashes/muzzle_flash_small_pink.xml` - Specifies the muzzle flash particle effect.
*   **`collide_with_entities`**: `0` - The projectile does not collide with entities in the standard sense (handled by `CellEaterComponent`).
*   **`friendly_fire`**: `1` - Can affect friendly entities.
*   **`config_explosion`**:
    *   `explosion_radius`: `1` - The radius of the explosion effect.
    *   `explosion_sprite`: `data/particles/black_hole_out.xml` - The visual sprite for the explosion.
    *   `hole_enabled`: `1` - Enables the "hole" effect of the black hole.
    *   `damage_mortals`: `1` - The explosion damages living entities.
    *   `sparks_enabled`: `1` - Sparks are generated by the explosion.
    *   `spark_material`: `plasma_fading_pink` - The material used for the sparks.

## Cell Eater Component

Handles the "eating" or destruction of cells/materials.

```xml
<CellEaterComponent
	radius="12"
	eat_dynamic_physics_bodies="1"
	ignored_material_tag="[indestructible]"
	>
</CellEaterComponent>
```

### Key Attributes:

*   **`radius`**: `12` - The area of effect for eating materials.
*   **`eat_dynamic_physics_bodies`**: `1` - The component will consume physics bodies.
*   **`ignored_material_tag`**: `[indestructible]` - Materials with this tag will not be eaten.
