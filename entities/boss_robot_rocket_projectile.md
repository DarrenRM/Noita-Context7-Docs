# Boss Robot Rocket Projectile

This document details the configuration of the rocket projectile fired by the boss robot in Noita.

## Core Entity Properties

*   **`name`**: `$projectile_default` - A generic name for projectile entities.
*   **`tags`**: `teleportable_NOT,projectile` - Indicates it cannot be teleported and is a projectile.

## Key Components

### VelocityComponent

Controls the projectile's movement physics.

*   **`gravity_y`**: `0` - The projectile is not affected by gravity.
*   **`mass`**: `0.1` - A low mass, contributing to its speed and responsiveness.

### HomingComponent

Enables the projectile to track its target.

*   **`target_tag`**: `prey` - The projectile will seek entities tagged as "prey".
*   **`homing_targeting_coeff`**: `15` - A high coefficient, indicating strong homing behavior.
*   **`detect_distance`**: `400` - The maximum distance at which the projectile can detect its target.
*   **`homing_velocity_multiplier`**: `1.0` - The projectile's velocity is not altered by the homing mechanism itself.

### ProjectileComponent

Defines the projectile's behavior, damage, and effects.

*   **`lob_min` / `lob_max`**: `0.8` / `1.0` - Controls the arc of the projectile.
*   **`speed_min` / `speed_max`**: `80` - The projectile travels at a constant speed of 80 units.
*   **`friction`**: `-1.0` - No friction applied, allowing it to maintain speed.
*   **`direction_random_rad`**: `0.05` - Slight randomness in its initial direction.
*   **`on_death_explode`**: `1` - The projectile explodes upon death.
*   **`on_lifetime_out_explode`**: `1` - The projectile explodes when its lifetime expires.
*   **`on_collision_die`**: `1` - The projectile is destroyed upon collision.
*   **`lifetime`**: `100` - The projectile exists for 100 frames before expiring.
*   **`damage`**: `6` - Base damage dealt by the projectile.
*   **`bounces_left`**: `1` - The projectile can bounce once.
*   **`knockback_force`**: `2.0` - Applies knockback to targets it hits.
*   **`muzzle_flash_file`**: `data/entities/particles/muzzle_flashes/muzzle_flash_launcher_large.xml` - Specifies the muzzle flash effect.
*   **`shoot_light_flash_r/g/b/radius`**: `255/210/40/90` - Defines the color and radius of the light flash when shot.

#### Damage by Type

*   **`fire`**: `2.2` - Deals 2.2 additional damage of type fire.

#### `config_explosion`

Details the explosion effect when the projectile detonates.

*   **`camera_shake`**: `20` - Significant camera shake upon explosion.
*   **`explosion_radius`**: `10` - The radius of the explosion.
*   **`explosion_sprite`**: `data/particles/explosion_032.xml` - The visual sprite for the explosion.
*   **`load_this_entity`**: `data/entities/particles/particle_explosion/main_small.xml` - The entity to load for the explosion effect.
*   **`ray_energy`**: `100000` - High energy for destruction.
*   **`damage`**: `5.5` - Damage dealt by the explosion itself.
*   **`damage_mortals`**: `1` - The explosion damages living entities.
*   **`physics_explosion_power.min/max`**: `0.5` / `0.7` - Controls the physics force of the explosion.
*   **`shake_vegetation`**: `1` - Causes vegetation to shake.
*   **`sparks_enabled`**: `1` - Sparks are generated by the explosion.
*   **`stains_enabled`**: `1` - Creates stains on surfaces.
*   **`audio_event_name`**: `explosions/tnt` - The sound event associated with the explosion.

### SpriteComponent

Defines the visual appearance of the projectile.

*   **`image_file`**: `data/projectiles_gfx/rocket.xml` - The graphical asset for the rocket.

### ParticleEmitterComponent (x2)

These components generate smoke and fire particles.

*   **`emitted_material_name`**: `smoke` / `fire` - Specifies the material of the particles.
*   **`count_min/max`**: Controls the number of particles emitted.
*   **`lifetime_min/max`**: Defines the duration particles exist.
*   **`x_vel_min/max`**, **`y_vel_min/max`**: Controls particle velocity.

### SpriteParticleEmitterComponent

Emits cosmetic smoke particles with specific behaviors.

*   **`sprite_file`**: `data/particles/smoke_orange.xml` - The sprite used for these particles.
*   **`scale_velocity.x/y`**: `-0.1` - Particles shrink over time.
*   **`randomize_rotation`**: Controls random initial rotation.
*   **`randomize_angular_velocity`**: Controls random initial rotation speed.
*   **`randomize_position`**: Controls random initial position offset.
*   **`render_back`**: `1` - Renders these particles behind other elements.

### AudioComponent

Plays sound effects associated with the projectile.

*   **`file`**: `data/audio/Desktop/projectiles.bank` - The audio bank containing the sounds.
*   **`event_root`**: `projectiles/rocket` - The base event name for projectile sounds.

### AudioLoopComponent

Plays a looping sound effect for the projectile.

*   **`file`**: `data/audio/Desktop/projectiles.bank` - The audio bank.
*   **`event_name`**: `projectiles/rocket_passby` - The specific looping sound event.
*   **`auto_play`**: `1` - The sound starts automatically.

### VariableStorageComponent

Stores variables for use by other game systems.

*   **`name`**: `projectile_file` - The name of the variable.
*   **`value_string`**: `data/entities/projectiles/rocket_tank.xml` - The value, likely referencing another projectile entity for more complex behavior or variations.
