# Glitter Bomb Shrapnel Projectile

This document describes the `glitter_bomb_shrapnel.xml` entity, which defines the behavior and appearance of the shrapnel projectiles created by the Glitter Bomb.

## Core Components

### Base Projectile (`<Base file="data/entities/base_projectile.xml">`)

This component inherits fundamental projectile properties.

*   **`VelocityComponent`**: Defines the physical movement characteristics.
    *   `gravity_y`: `120` - Applies downward gravitational pull.
    *   `air_friction`: `2.0` - Resistance to movement in the air.
    *   `mass`: `0.09` - The projectile's mass.

### Initial Velocity (`<SetStartVelocityComponent>`)

Determines how the projectile is launched.

*   `randomize_speed.min`: `80` - Minimum launch speed.
*   `randomize_speed.max`: `120` - Maximum launch speed.
*   `randomize_angle.min`: `0` - Minimum launch angle (radians).
*   `randomize_angle.max`: `6.28319` - Maximum launch angle (radians, full circle).

### Projectile Behavior (`<ProjectileComponent>`)

Governs the projectile's lifecycle and interactions.

*   `speed_min`/`speed_max`: `0` - These are set to 0, indicating the initial velocity is handled by `SetStartVelocityComponent`.
*   `damage`: `0` - This shrapnel itself does no direct damage.
*   `lifetime`: `200` - The number of frames the projectile exists before expiring.
*   `die_on_low_velocity`: `0` - The projectile does not die simply due to slowing down.
*   `on_death_explode`: `1` - The projectile explodes when it dies.
*   `on_death_gfx_leave_sprite`: `0` - No sprite is left behind when it dies.
*   `on_lifetime_out_explode`: `1` - Explodes when its lifetime expires.
*   `on_collision_die`: `1` - Dies upon colliding with something.

#### Explosion Configuration (`<config_explosion>`)

Details of the explosion that occurs upon death.

*   `damage`: `0.5` - The damage dealt by the explosion.
*   `explosion_radius`: `10` - The area of effect for the explosion.
*   `explosion_sprite`: `data/particles/explosion_012_poof.xml` - The visual effect for the explosion.
*   `spark_material`: `spark_purple_bright` - The type of sparks generated by the explosion.
*   `audio_event_name`: `explosion_small` - The sound effect played for the explosion.

### Visuals (`<SpriteComponent>`)

Defines the projectile's appearance.

*   `image_file`: `data/projectiles_gfx/glitter_bomb_shrapnel.xml` - Specifies the sprite sheet or definition for the shrapnel's visual.
*   `additive`: `1` - Enables additive blending for the sprite, making it appear brighter.

## Particle Emitters (FX)

### Smoke Cloud Emitter (`<SpriteParticleEmitterComponent>`)

Generates a small smoke effect.

*   `sprite_file`: `data/particles/smoke_cloud_tiny_glitter_1.xml` - The particle sprite to emit.
*   `emission_interval_min_frames`/`max_frames`: `2` - Emits a particle every 2 frames.
*   `additive`: `1` - Additive blending for the smoke.
*   `scale.x`/`scale.y`: `1.0` - Base scale of the particles.
*   `randomize_scale`: Varies the scale slightly for a more natural look.
*   `randomize_velocity.min_y`/`max_y`: `-10` to `0` - Particles move upwards slightly.

### Glitter Spark Emitter (`<SpriteParticleEmitterComponent>`)

Generates the characteristic glitter sparks.

*   `sprite_file`: `data/particles/spark_glitter.xml` - The particle sprite for glitter.
*   `emission_interval_min_frames`/`max_frames`: `2` to `16` - Emission rate varies.
*   `additive`: `1` - Additive blending for sparks.
*   `emissive`: `1` - Sparks glow.
*   `randomize_lifetime`: `0.5` to `2.0` - Sparks have a short lifespan.
*   `randomize_velocity`: Varies the direction and speed of sparks.
*   `randomize_position`: `min_x`/`max_x`, `min_y`/`max_y`: `-6` to `6` - Sparks originate from a small area around the projectile.

## Audio (`<AudioLoopComponent>`)

*   `file`: `data/audio/Desktop/projectiles.bank` - The audio bank containing the sound event.
*   `event_name`: `player_projectiles/bomb_glitter/loop` - The specific sound event to play.
*   `auto_play`: `1` - The sound starts automatically when the projectile is created.

## Variable Storage (`<VariableStorageComponent>`)

*   `name`: `projectile_file` - Stores the path to this entity's XML file.
*   `value_string`: `data/entities/projectiles/deck/glitter_bomb_shrapnel.xml` - The actual file path.
