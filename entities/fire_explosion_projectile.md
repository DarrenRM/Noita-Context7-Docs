# Fire Explosion Projectile

This entity defines a projectile that explodes upon death or when its lifetime expires, creating a fire effect. It's designed to be a simple, self-contained explosion rather than a projectile with direct impact damage.

## Key Components

### `ProjectileComponent`

This is the core component that governs the projectile's behavior.
