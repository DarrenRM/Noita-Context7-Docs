# Soldier Shot Projectile

This document details the configuration of the "soldiershot.xml" projectile entity in Noita, designed for AI-assisted modding.

## Core Components

### Base Projectile (`<Base file="data/entities/base_projectile.xml">`)

This section inherits fundamental projectile properties.

*   **`VelocityComponent`**:
    *   `gravity_y="0"`: The projectile is not affected by gravity.
    *   `air_friction="-2"`: Minimal air resistance.
    *   `mass="0.05"`: Light mass.

### Projectile Component (`<ProjectileComponent>`)

Defines the primary behavior and characteristics of the projectile.

*   **`lob_min="0.8"`, `lob_max="1.0"`**: Controls the arc of the projectile.
*   **`speed_min="150"`, `speed_max="150"`**: The projectile travels at a constant speed of 150 units.
*   **`die_on_low_velocity="0"`**: The projectile does not die if its velocity drops.
*   **`on_death_explode="1"`**: The projectile explodes upon death.
*   **`on_lifetime_out_explode="1"`**: The projectile explodes when its lifetime expires.
*   **`damage="0.6"`**: Base damage dealt by the projectile.
*   **`on_collision_die="1"`**: The projectile dies upon colliding with an entity.
*   **`lifetime="100"`**: The projectile exists for 100 frames before expiring.
*   **`knockback_force="1.0"`**: Applies a knockback force of 1.0.
*   **`muzzle_flash_file="data/entities/particles/muzzle_flashes/muzzle_flash_magic_launcher_blue.xml"`**: Specifies the muzzle flash particle effect.
*   **`shoot_light_flash_r="50"`, `shoot_light_flash_g="125"`, `shoot_light_flash_b="235"`, `shoot_light_flash_radius="100"`**: Defines the color and radius of the light flash when the projectile is fired.

#### Damage by Type (`<damage_by_type>`)

Specifies additional damage multipliers for specific damage types.

| Type      | Damage |
| :-------- | :----- |
| `fire`    | `0.4`  |
| `slice`   | `0.4`  |
| `radioactive` | `0.4`  |

#### Explosion Configuration (`<config_explosion>`)

Details the properties of the explosion when the projectile dies.

*   **`camera_shake="10"`**: The intensity of camera shake upon explosion.
*   **`explosion_radius="20"`**: The radius of the explosion.
*   **`explosion_sprite="data/particles/explosion_016_plasma.xml"`**: The particle effect used for the explosion.
*   **`ray_energy="25000"`**: The energy of any rays generated by the explosion.
*   **`damage="0.5"`**: Damage dealt by the explosion itself.
*   **`physics_explosion_power.min="0.15"`, `physics_explosion_power.max="0.35"`**: The minimum and maximum force of the physics-based explosion.
*   **`sparks_count_min="4"`, `sparks_count_max="10"`**: The range for the number of sparks generated.
*   **`spark_material="plasma_fading"`**: The material used for the sparks.
*   **`stains_enabled="1"`, `stains_radius="12"`**: Enables and sets the radius for impact stains.

### Homing Component (`<HomingComponent>`)

Enables homing behavior for the projectile.

*   **`homing_targeting_coeff="20.0"`**: The strength of the homing targeting.
*   **`homing_velocity_multiplier="0.99"`**: How much the homing affects the projectile's velocity.
*   **`detect_distance="60"`**: The distance at which the projectile can detect targets.

### Sprite Component (`<SpriteComponent>`)

Defines the visual representation of the projectile.

*   **`image_file="data/projectiles_gfx/fireball_smaller_blue.xml"`**: The graphical asset used for the projectile.
*   **`rect_animation="fireball"`**: Specifies the animation to use.
*   **`additive="1"`**: Enables additive blending for the sprite, often used for glowing effects.

### Audio Component (`<AudioComponent>`)

Manages the sound effects associated with the projectile.

*   **`file="data/audio/Desktop/projectiles.bank"`**: The audio bank containing the sound events.
*   **`event_root="projectiles/laser"`**: The root event path for projectile sounds.

### Particle Emitter Components (`<ParticleEmitterComponent>`)

These components generate visual particle effects.

#### Trail Particles

*   **`emitted_material_name="spark_blue"`**: The material of the particles emitted.
*   **`is_trail="1"`**: Indicates this emitter creates a trail effect.
*   **`lifetime_min="0.1"`, `lifetime_max="0.2"`**: The duration of each particle.
*   **`emission_interval_min_frames="1"`, `emission_interval_max_frames="2"`**: Controls the rate of particle emission.

#### Explosion/Impact Particles

*   **`emitted_material_name="plasma_fading"`**: The material of the particles emitted.
*   **`lifetime_min="0.2"`, `lifetime_max="0.6"`**: The duration of each particle.
*   **`x_vel_min="20"`, `x_vel_max="80"`**: The horizontal velocity range of the particles.

### Light Component (`<LightComponent>`)

Adds a light source to the projectile.

*   **`radius="150"`**: The radius of the light.
*   **`r="30"`, `g="90"`, `b="30"`**: The RGB color of the light (a greenish-blue hue).

### Variable Storage Component (`<VariableStorageComponent>`)

Stores custom variables for the entity.

*   **`name="projectile_file"`**: The name of the variable.
*   **`value_string="data/entities/projectiles/soldiershot.xml"`**: The value of the variable, referencing its own file path.
