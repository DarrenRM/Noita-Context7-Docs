### `ProjectileComponent` Attributes:

*   **`lifetime`**: `80` - The duration in frames before the projectile explodes if it doesn't collide.
*   **`damage`**: `0` - Base damage of the projectile itself (before explosion).
*   **`collide_with_world`**: `0` - The projectile does not collide with the environment.
*   **`on_lifetime_out_explode`**: `1` - The projectile will explode when its lifetime ends.
*   **`on_death_explode`**: `1` - The projectile will explode when it dies for any reason (e.g., collision).
*   **`on_collision_die`**: `0` - The projectile does not die on collision, it will still explode.
*   **`explosion_dont_damage_shooter`**: `1` - The explosion will not damage the entity that fired it.
*   **`shoot_light_flash_radius`**: `120` - Radius of the light flash effect when the projectile is fired.
*   **`shoot_light_flash_r`, `shoot_light_flash_g`, `shoot_light_flash_b`**: `255`, `240`, `30` - RGB values for the light flash color (yellowish).

#### `config_explosion` Sub-attributes:

*   **`damage`**: `8` - Damage dealt by the explosion.
*   **`camera_shake`**: `40` - Intensity of camera shake caused by the explosion.
*   **`explosion_radius`**: `40` - The radius of the explosion's effect.
*   **`explosion_sprite`**: `data/particles/explosion_128.xml` - The visual effect used for the explosion.
*   **`load_this_entity`**: `data/entities/misc/loose_ground.xml` - An entity to spawn at the explosion's center (in this case, loose ground).
*   **`create_cell_probability`**: `40` - Percentage chance to create a cell (e.g., loose ground) at the explosion's origin.
*   **`hole_enabled`**: `1` - The explosion can create holes in terrain.
*   **`ray_energy`**: `64000` - Energy value for any ray effects generated by the explosion.
*   **`particle_effect`**: `1` - Enables particle effects for the explosion.
*   **`damage_mortals`**: `1` - The explosion damages living entities.
*   **`physics_explosion_power.min`, `physics_explosion_power.max`**: `2.5`, `6` - Minimum and maximum force applied to physics objects by the explosion.
*   **`shake_vegetation`**: `1` - The explosion will shake vegetation.
*   **`sparks_count_min`, `sparks_count_max`**: `800`, `500` - Range for the number of sparks generated by the explosion.
*   **`stains_enabled`**: `1` - Enables stain effects from the explosion.
*   **`stains_radius`**: `25` - Radius of the stains.
*   **`background_lightning_count`**: `5` - Number of background lightning effects to spawn.
*   **`max_durability_to_destroy`**: `12` - Maximum durability of objects that can be destroyed by the explosion.
*   **`audio_event_name`**: `explosions/nuke` - The sound event triggered by the explosion.

### ParticleEmitterComponent

This component is responsible for the visual buildup effect before the projectile explodes.

*   **`_tags`**: `melee_buildup_particles` - A tag for identifying these particles.
*   **`emitted_material_name`**: `spark_yellow` - The material used for the emitted particles.
*   **`gravity.y`**: `0.0` - Particles have no vertical gravity.
*   **`lifetime_min`, `lifetime_max`**: `0.1`, `1.5` - The lifespan of individual particles.
*   **`count_min`, `count_max`**: `20`, `20` - The number of particles emitted per emission.
*   **`render_on_grid`**: `1` - Particles are rendered on the game grid.
*   **`fade_based_on_lifetime`**: `1` - Particles fade out as their lifetime ends.
*   **`area_circle_radius.min`, `area_circle_radius.max`**: `0`, `0` - Particles are emitted from a single point.
*   **`airflow_force`, `airflow_time`, `airflow_scale`**: `4.1`, `1.01`, `0.05` - Parameters controlling airflow effects on particles.
*   **`emission_interval_min_frames`, `emission_interval_max_frames`**: `1`, `1` - Particles are emitted continuously.
*   **`emit_cosmetic_particles`**: `1` - Emits cosmetic particles.
*   **`image_animation_file`**: `data/particles/image_emitters/circle_reverse_128.png` - The texture used for particle animation.
*   **`image_animation_speed`**: `4` - Speed of the particle animation.
*   **`image_animation_loop`**: `0` - The animation does not loop.
*   **`is_emitting`**: `1` - The particle emitter is active.

### AudioLoopComponent

Plays a looping sound effect for the projectile.

*   **`file`**: `data/audio/Desktop/projectiles.bank` - The audio bank containing the sound.
*   **`event_name`**: `projectiles/fuse_burn_slow` - The specific sound event to play.
*   **`auto_play`**: `1` - The sound starts playing automatically when the entity is spawned.

### LuaComponent

Attaches a Lua script to control more complex behaviors.

*   **`script_source_file`**: `data/entities/animals/boss_centipede/melee_shots.lua` - The path to the Lua script.
*   **`execute_every_n_frame`**: `-1` - The script is not executed on a fixed frame interval.
*   **`execute_on_removed`**: `1` - The script's `on_remove` function will be called when the entity is removed.

### VariableStorageComponent

Stores a variable for potential use by other entities or scripts.

*   **`name`**: `projectile_file` - The name of the variable.
*   **`value_string`**: `data/entities/animals/boss_centipede/melee.xml` - The value of the variable, pointing to this entity's XML file.
