# Death Cross Projectile

This document details the configuration of the "Death Cross" projectile entity in Noita, focusing on its behavior, visual effects, and damage properties for AI-assisted modding.

## Core Entity Configuration

The `Entity` tag defines the fundamental properties of the projectile.

```xml
<Entity
  name="$projectile_default" tags="projectile_player,death_cross">
  <!-- ... other components ... -->
</Entity>
```

*   **`name`**: `$projectile_default` - Indicates this entity inherits from a default projectile template.
*   **`tags`**: `projectile_player,death_cross` - Essential for identifying this projectile in game logic and for targeting.

## Base Projectile Properties

The `BaseComponent` inherits common projectile behaviors.

```xml
<Base file="data/entities/base_projectile.xml">
  <VelocityComponent
    gravity_y="0"
    air_friction="0"
    mass="0.08">
  </VelocityComponent>
</Base>
```

*   **`VelocityComponent`**:
    *   `gravity_y="0"`: The projectile is not affected by gravity.
    *   `air_friction="0"`: No air resistance.
    *   `mass="0.08"`: A small mass value.

## Homing Behavior

The `HomingComponent` enables the projectile to track targets.

```xml
<HomingComponent
  target_tag="prey"
  homing_targeting_coeff="3"
  detect_distance="350"
  homing_velocity_multiplier="1.0">
</HomingComponent>
```

*   **`target_tag`**: `prey` - The projectile will actively seek entities tagged as "prey".
*   **`homing_targeting_coeff`**: `3` - Controls the strength of the homing behavior.
*   **`detect_distance`**: `350` - The maximum range at which the projectile can detect targets.

## Projectile Mechanics

The `ProjectileComponent` governs the projectile's core functionality.

```xml
<ProjectileComponent
  _enabled="1"
  lob_min="0.8"
  lob_max="1.0"
  speed_min="0"
  speed_max="0"
  die_on_low_velocity="0"
  on_death_explode="1"
  on_death_gfx_leave_sprite="0"
  on_lifetime_out_explode="1"
  explosion_dont_damage_shooter="0"
  damage="0.0"
  on_collision_die="0"
  bounces_left="20"
  lifetime="68"
  shoot_light_flash_r="35"
  shoot_light_flash_g="210"
  shoot_light_flash_b="255"
  shoot_light_flash_radius="90"
  knockback_force="1.6"
  damage_every_x_frames="15">
  <!-- ... explosion config ... -->
</ProjectileComponent>
```

*   **`lob_min`/`lob_max`**: `0.8` to `1.0` - Affects the initial trajectory arc.
*   **`speed_min`/`speed_max`**: `0` - The projectile does not have an initial speed.
*   **`die_on_low_velocity`**: `0` - The projectile will not die if its velocity drops.
*   **`on_death_explode`**: `1` - The projectile explodes upon death.
*   **`on_lifetime_out_explode`**: `1` - The projectile explodes when its lifetime expires.
*   **`bounces_left`**: `20` - The projectile can bounce up to 20 times.
*   **`lifetime`**: `68` - The projectile exists for 68 frames.
*   **`knockback_force`**: `1.6` - The force applied to entities upon collision.
*   **`damage_every_x_frames`**: `15` - Damage is applied every 15 frames.

### Explosion Configuration

The `config_explosion` sub-element defines the properties of the explosion.

```xml
<config_explosion
  never_cache="1"
  camera_shake="30.5"
  explosion_radius="25"
  explosion_sprite="data/particles/explosion_032_plasma.xml"
  create_cell_material="plasma_fading"
  ray_energy="50000"
  hole_destroy_liquid="1"
  hole_enabled="1"
  damage="3"
  physics_explosion_power.min="1.2"
  physics_explosion_power.max="2.2"
  physics_throw_enabled="1"
  sparks_enabled="1"
  spark_material="spark_blue"
  light_r="15"
  light_g="180"
  light_b="250"
  audio_event_name="explosions/magic_rocket_mid">
</config_explosion>
```

*   **`camera_shake`**: `30.5` - Intensity of camera shake upon explosion.
*   **`explosion_radius`**: `25` - The radius of the explosion effect.
*   **`explosion_sprite`**: `data/particles/explosion_032_plasma.xml` - The visual effect used for the explosion.
*   **`create_cell_material`**: `plasma_fading` - The material created by the explosion.
*   **`damage`**: `3` - The base damage dealt by the explosion.
*   **`physics_explosion_power.min`/`max`**: `1.2` to `2.2` - Controls the physics force of the explosion.
*   **`sparks_enabled`**: `1` - Sparks are generated by the explosion.
*   **`spark_material`**: `spark_blue` - The material used for sparks.
*   **`audio_event_name`**: `explosions/magic_rocket_mid` - The sound effect played on explosion.

## Visual Components

### Sprite Component

```xml
<SpriteComponent
  _enabled="0"
  alpha="1"
  image_file="data/projectiles_gfx/glow.xml"
  rect_animation="fireball"
  additive="1"
  update_transform_rotation="0">
</SpriteComponent>
```

*   **`_enabled`**: `0` - This sprite is disabled by default.
*   **`image_file`**: `data/projectiles_gfx/glow.xml` - The graphical asset used.
*   **`rect_animation`**: `fireball` - Specifies the animation to use.
*   **`additive`**: `1` - Enables additive blending for the sprite.

### Light Component

```xml
<LightComponent
  _enabled="1"
  radius="250"
  r="40"
  g="185"
  b="250">
</LightComponent>
```

*   **`_enabled`**: `1` - The light effect is active.
*   **`radius`**: `250` - The radius of the light emitted.
*   **`r`, `g`, `b`**: `40, 185, 250` - The color of the light (a bluish-white).

## Particle Emitters

Two `ParticleEmitterComponent` instances are used to generate cosmetic particles.

```xml
<ParticleEmitterComponent
  emitted_material_name="plasma_fading"
  x_vel_min="-60"
  x_vel_max="60"
  y_vel_min="0"
  y_vel_max="0"
  gravity.y="0"
  count_min="5"
  count_max="9"
  lifetime_min="0.3"
  lifetime_max="1.6"
  create_real_particles="0"
  emit_cosmetic_particles="1"
  fade_based_on_lifetime="1"
  emission_interval_min_frames="1"
  emission_interval_max_frames="1"
  is_emitting="1">
</ParticleEmitterComponent>

<ParticleEmitterComponent
  emitted_material_name="plasma_fading"
  x_vel_min="0"
  x_vel_max="0"
  y_vel_min="-60"
  y_vel_max="60"
  gravity.y="0"
  count_min="5"
  count_max="9"
  lifetime_min="0.3"
  lifetime_max="1.6"
  create_real_particles="0"
  emit_cosmetic_particles="1"
  fade_based_on_lifetime="1"
  emission_interval_min_frames="1"
  emission_interval_max_frames="1"
  is_emitting="1">
</ParticleEmitterComponent>
```

*   **`emitted_material_name`**: `plasma_fading` - The type of particles emitted.
*   **`x_vel_min`/`max`, `y_vel_min`/`max`**: Define the velocity range for emitted particles. One emitter focuses on horizontal, the other on vertical.
*   **`count_min`/`max`**: `5` to `9` - The number of particles emitted per burst.
*   **`lifetime_min`/`max`**: `0.3` to `1.6` - The duration particles exist.
*   **`emit_cosmetic_particles`**: `1` - These are purely visual effects.
*   **`fade_based_on_lifetime`**: `1` - Particles fade out as their lifetime decreases.
