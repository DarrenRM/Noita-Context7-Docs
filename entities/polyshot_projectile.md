# Polyshot Projectile

This document details the configuration of the "polyshot" projectile entity in Noita, focusing on its behavior, visual representation, and effects.

## Base Projectile Configuration

The projectile inherits fundamental properties from `base_projectile.xml`.

### Velocity Component

*   **`gravity_y`**: `10` - Controls the downward acceleration due to gravity.
*   **`mass`**: `0.07` - Affects how the projectile interacts with physics and forces.

## Projectile Component

This component defines the core mechanics of the projectile.

### Key Attributes

*   **`speed_min`**: `130` - The minimum initial velocity of the projectile.
*   **`speed_max`**: `160` - The maximum initial velocity of the projectile.
*   **`on_death_explode`**: `1` - The projectile will explode upon death.
*   **`on_lifetime_out_explode`**: `1` - The projectile will explode when its lifetime expires.
*   **`on_collision_die`**: `1` - The projectile will be destroyed upon collision.
*   **`die_on_low_velocity`**: `1` - The projectile will be destroyed if its velocity drops too low.
*   **`damage`**: `0` - Base damage dealt by the projectile itself.
*   **`lifetime`**: `330` - The duration in frames before the projectile expires.
*   **`knockback_force`**: `2.0` - The force applied to entities it hits.

### Damage Types

*   **`holy`**: `1.5` - Additional damage multiplier for holy damage types.

### Explosion Configuration (`config_explosion`)

This section defines the properties of the explosion that occurs when the projectile dies or its lifetime ends.

*   **`never_cache`**: `1` - Prevents the explosion from being cached, ensuring unique instances.
*   **`damage`**: `0` - Damage dealt by the explosion itself.
*   **`camera_shake`**: `4.5` - The intensity of camera shake upon explosion.
*   **`explosion_radius`**: `9` - The radius of the explosion effect.
*   **`explosion_sprite`**: `data/particles/explosion_032_pink.xml` - The visual sprite used for the explosion.
*   **`explosion_sprite_emissive`**: `1` - Makes the explosion sprite emissive.
*   **`explosion_sprite_additive`**: `1` - Uses additive blending for the explosion sprite.
*   **`create_cell_material`**: `magic_liquid_random_polymorph` - The material created in cells affected by the explosion.
*   **`create_cell_probability`**: `40` - The percentage chance to create the specified cell material.
*   **`hole_enabled`**: `1` - Enables the creation of holes in terrain.
*   **`hole_image`**: `data/temp/explosion_hole.png` - The sprite used for the explosion hole.
*   **`audio_event_name`**: `explosions/tnt` - The sound event triggered by the explosion.
*   **`physics_explosion_power.max`**: `0.4` - The maximum power of the physics-based explosion effect.
*   **`shake_vegetation`**: `1` - Causes vegetation to shake during the explosion.
*   **`sparks_count_max`**: `20` - The maximum number of sparks generated by the explosion.
*   **`stains_enabled`**: `1` - Enables the creation of stains from the explosion.
*   **`stains_radius`**: `9` - The radius of the stains created.
*   **`audio_enabled`**: `1` - Enables audio for the explosion.

## Sprite Component

Defines the visual appearance of the projectile.

*   **`image_file`**: `data/projectiles_gfx/polyshot.xml` - The file containing the projectile's sprite animations.
*   **`emissive`**: `1` - Makes the projectile sprite emissive.
*   **`additive`**: `1` - Uses additive blending for the projectile sprite.

## Particle Emitter Component

Responsible for generating cosmetic particles.

*   **`emitted_material_name`**: `magic_liquid_random_polymorph` - The material of the particles emitted.
*   **`count_min` / `count_max`**: Varies, indicating the number of particles emitted per interval.
*   **`lifetime_min` / `lifetime_max`**: `0.1` / `0.3` - The duration particles exist.
*   **`create_real_particles`**: `1` - Creates actual game particles.
*   **`emit_cosmetic_particles`**: `0` - Does not emit purely cosmetic particles.
*   **`emission_interval_min_frames` / `emission_interval_max_frames`**: `5` / `8` - The frame interval between particle emissions.
*   **`is_emitting`**: `1` - The particle emitter is active.

## Hitbox Component

Defines the collision area of the projectile.

*   **`aabb_min_x` / `aabb_max_x`**: `-5` / `5` - The bounding box dimensions in the X-axis.
*   **`aabb_min_y` / `aabb_max_y`**: `-5` / `5` - The bounding box dimensions in the Y-axis.

## Damage Model Component

Governs how the projectile takes damage and interacts with damageable entities.

*   **`hp`**: `0.6` - The health points of the projectile.
*   **`blood_material`**: `magic_liquid_random_polymorph` - The material that appears when the projectile is damaged.
*   **`blood_multiplier`**: `0` - No multiplier for blood material.

## Audio Component

Handles sound effects for the projectile.

*   **`file`**: `data/audio/Desktop/projectiles.bank` - The audio bank containing projectile sounds.
*   **`event_root`**: `projectiles/bullet_fire_heavy` - The root event name for projectile sounds.

## Variable Storage Component

Stores custom variables for the entity.

*   **`name`**: `projectile_file` - The name of the variable.
*   **`value_string`**: `data/entities/projectiles/polyshot.xml` - The string value, referencing its own file.
