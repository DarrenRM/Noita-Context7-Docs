# Radioactive Orb Projectile

This document describes the `orb_mat_radioactive.xml` entity, which represents a radioactive orb projectile used by the boss centipede in Noita. It details its key attributes and components for AI-assisted modding.

## Entity Definition

The core entity is defined with the tag `hittable`, indicating it can be targeted and damaged.

```xml
<Entity tags="hittable"
  name="$projectile_default"
>
  <!-- ... components ... -->
</Entity>
```

## Key Components and Attributes

### Base Projectile Component

This component inherits from the base projectile functionality, defining its physical properties.

*   **`gravity_y="0"`**: The projectile is not affected by gravity.
*   **`air_friction="1"`**: Maximum air friction is applied.
*   **`mass="0.05"`**: A small mass for the projectile.

```xml
<Base file="data/entities/base_projectile.xml" >
    <VelocityComponent
        gravity_y="0"
        air_friction="1"
        mass="0.05"
    >
    </VelocityComponent>
</Base>
```

### Projectile Component

This is the primary component defining the projectile's behavior.

*   **`lob_min="0.8"`, `lob_max="1.0"`**: Controls the lobbing behavior, with a high probability of a straight trajectory.
*   **`speed_min="40"`, `speed_max="60"`**: Defines the projectile's speed range.
*   **`die_on_low_velocity="0"`**: The projectile does not die when its velocity becomes low.
*   **`on_death_explode="1"`**: The projectile explodes upon death.
*   **`on_lifetime_out_explode="1"`**: The projectile explodes when its lifetime expires.
*   **`explosion_dont_damage_shooter="1"`**: The explosion does not damage the entity that fired it.
*   **`damage="0"`**: The projectile itself deals no direct damage.
*   **`on_collision_die="0"`**: The projectile does not die upon collision.
*   **`lifetime="400"`**: The projectile's lifespan in frames.
*   **`knockback_force="1.0"`**: The force applied to knock back entities.

#### `config_explosion` Sub-component

This nested component defines the properties of the explosion that occurs upon the projectile's death or lifetime expiry.

*   **`never_cache="1"`**: Ensures the explosion is not cached, allowing for dynamic effects.
*   **`camera_shake="0"`**: No camera shake is applied.
*   **`explosion_radius="16"`**: The radius of the explosion.
*   **`explosion_sprite="data/particles/explosion_016_plasma_pink.xml"`**: The visual sprite for the explosion.
*   **`create_cell_probability="100"`**: Guarantees the creation of cells upon explosion.
*   **`create_cell_material="radioactive_liquid"`**: The material of the created cells is radioactive liquid.
*   **`ray_energy="5000"`**: The energy of the explosion's rays.
*   **`hole_destroy_liquid="1"`**: The explosion destroys liquids.
*   **`hole_enabled="1"`**: The creation of holes by the explosion is enabled.
*   **`hole_image="data/temp/explosion_hole.png"`**: The image used for the explosion hole.
*   **`explosion_sprite_emissive="1"`, `explosion_sprite_additive="1"`**: Properties for the explosion sprite's rendering.
*   **`particle_effect="0"`**: No specific particle effect is generated by the explosion itself.
*   **`damage_mortals="0"`**: The explosion does not damage mortal entities.
*   **`physics_explosion_power.min="0.13"`, `physics_explosion_power.max="0.23"`**: The minimum and maximum physics force of the explosion.
*   **`physics_throw_enabled="1"`**: Physics-based throwing is enabled for the explosion.
*   **`shake_vegetation="1"`**: The explosion shakes vegetation.
*   **`light_r="15"`, `light_g="15"`, `light_b="40"`**: The RGB values for the explosion's light.

```xml
<ProjectileComponent
    _enabled="1"
    lob_min="0.8"
    lob_max="1.0"
    speed_min="40"
    speed_max="60"
    die_on_low_velocity="0"
    on_death_explode="1"
    on_death_gfx_leave_sprite="0"
    on_lifetime_out_explode="1"
    explosion_dont_damage_shooter="1"
    damage="0"
    on_collision_die="0"
    lifetime="400"
    knockback_force="1.0"
>
    <config_explosion
      never_cache="1"
      camera_shake="0"
      explosion_radius="16"
      explosion_sprite="data/particles/explosion_016_plasma_pink.xml"
      explosion_sprite_lifetime="0.0"
      create_cell_probability="100"
      create_cell_material="radioactive_liquid"
      ray_energy="5000"
      hole_destroy_liquid="1"
      hole_enabled="1"
      hole_image="data/temp/explosion_hole.png"
      explosion_sprite_emissive="1"
      explosion_sprite_additive="1"
      particle_effect="0"
      damage_mortals="0"
      physics_explosion_power.min="0.13"
      physics_explosion_power.max="0.23"
      physics_throw_enabled="1"
      shake_vegetation="1"
      sparks_enabled="0"
      sparks_count_max="0"
      sparks_count_min="0"
      light_fade_time="0.8"
      light_r="15"
      light_g="15"
      light_b="40"
      stains_enabled="0"
      stains_image="data/temp/explosion_stain.png" >
    </config_explosion>
</ProjectileComponent>
```

### Particle Emitter Component

This component generates cosmetic green sparks.

*   **`emitted_material_name="spark_green"`**: The material of the emitted particles.
*   **`gravity.y="0.0"`**: Particles are not affected by gravity.
*   **`lifetime_min="3.5"`, `lifetime_max="7.5"`**: The lifespan range of the particles.
*   **`count_min="2"`, `count_max="4"`**: The number of particles emitted per emission.
*   **`render_on_grid="1"`**: Particles are rendered on the game grid.
*   **`fade_based_on_lifetime="1"`**: Particles fade out as their lifetime decreases.
*   **`area_circle_radius.max="10"`**: The maximum radius of the emission area.
*   **`cosmetic_force_create="0"`**: Particles are not created with cosmetic force.
*   **`airflow_force="0.5"`, `airflow_time="0.01"`, `airflow_scale="0.05"`**: Parameters for airflow affecting particles.
*   **`emission_interval_min_frames="1"`, `emission_interval_max_frames="1"`**: Particles are emitted every frame.
*   **`emit_cosmetic_particles="1"`**: Cosmetic particles are emitted.
*   **`is_emitting="1"`**: The emitter is active.

```xml
<ParticleEmitterComponent
    emitted_material_name="spark_green"
    gravity.y="0.0"
    lifetime_min="3.5"
    lifetime_max="7.5"
    count_min="2"
    count_max="4"
    render_on_grid="1"
    fade_based_on_lifetime="1"
    area_circle_radius.max="10"
    cosmetic_force_create="0"
    airflow_force="0.5"
    airflow_time="0.01"
    airflow_scale="0.05"
    emission_interval_min_frames="1"
    emission_interval_max_frames="1"
    emit_cosmetic_particles="1"
    is_emitting="1" >
</ParticleEmitterComponent>
```

*   **`image_file="data/entities/animals/boss_centipede/orb_mat_radioactive_gfx.xml"`**: The file containing the projectile's graphics.
*   **`rect_animation="spawn"`**: Specifies the "spawn" animation to be used.
*   **`z_index="0.5"`**: Controls the rendering layer.

```xml
<SpriteComponent

    image_file="data/entities/animals/boss_centipede/orb_mat_radioactive_gfx.xml"
    next_rect_animation=""
    offset_x="0"
    offset_y="0"
    rect_animation="spawn"
    z_index="0.5"
>
</SpriteComponent>
```

### Light Component

Adds a light source to the projectile.

*   **`radius="150"`**: The radius of the light.
*   **`r="130"`, `g="35"`, `b="90"`**: The RGB color of the light.

```xml
<LightComponent
    _enabled="1"
    radius="150"
    r="130"
    g="35"
    b="90">
</LightComponent>
```

### Audio Component

Specifies the sound bank and event root for projectile sounds.

*   **`file="data/audio/Desktop/projectiles.bank"`**: The audio bank file.
*   **`event_root="projectiles/magic"`**: The root event for magic projectile sounds.

```xml
<AudioComponent
    file="data/audio/Desktop/projectiles.bank"
    event_root="projectiles/magic" >
</AudioComponent>
```

### Lua Component

Attaches a Lua script for custom behavior.

*   **`script_source_file="data/entities/animals/boss_centipede/orb_mat_animate.lua"`**: The path to the Lua script.
*   **`remove_after_executed="1"`**: The script is removed after execution.
*   **`execute_every_n_frame="300"`**: The script executes every 300 frames.

```xml
<LuaComponent
    script_source_file="data/entities/animals/boss_centipede/orb_mat_animate.lua"
    remove_after_executed="1"
    execute_every_n_frame="300"
>
</LuaComponent>
```

### Hitbox Component

Defines the collision area of the projectile.

*   **`aabb_min_x="-9"`, `aabb_max_x="9"`**: The minimum and maximum X-axis bounds.
*   **`aabb_min_y="-9"`, `aabb_max_y="9"`**: The minimum and maximum Y-axis bounds.

```xml
<HitboxComponent
    _enabled="1"
    aabb_min_x="-9"
    aabb_max_x="9"
    aabb_min_y="-9"
    aabb_max_y="9" >
</HitboxComponent>
```

### Damage Model Component

Defines how the projectile interacts with damage and materials.

*   **`hp="0.6"`**: The projectile has a very low HP, making it easily destroyed.
*   **`fire_probability_of_ignition="0"`**: Does not ignite on fire.
*   **`falling_damages="0"`**: Does not take damage from falling.
*   **`air_needed="0"`**: Does not require air.
*   **`materials_damage="0"`**: Does not damage materials directly.
*   **`blood_material="radioactive_liquid"`**: When damaged, it leaves radioactive liquid.
*   **`blood_multiplier="0"`**: No multiplier for blood.
*   **`create_ragdoll="0"`**: Does not create a ragdoll upon destruction.

```xml
<DamageModelComponent
    _enabled="1"
    hp="0.6"
    fire_probability_of_ignition="0"
    falling_damages="0"
    air_needed="0"
    materials_damage="0"
    ragdoll_filenames_file=""
    blood_material="radioactive_liquid"
    blood_multiplier="0"
    create_ragdoll="0" >
</DamageModelComponent>
```
