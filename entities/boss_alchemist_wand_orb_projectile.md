# Boss Alchemist Wand Orb Projectile

This document details the `wand_orb.xml` entity, which defines the projectile fired by the Boss Alchemist. It's a specialized projectile with unique behaviors and visual effects.

## Core Components

### Base Projectile (`Base`)

*   **`VelocityComponent`**:
    *   `gravity_y="0"`: The projectile is not affected by gravity.
    *   `air_friction="-0.1"`: Minimal air resistance.
    *   `mass="0.055"`: Light mass.

### Projectile Behavior (`ProjectileComponent`)

*   **`lob_min="0.8"`, `lob_max="1.0"`**: Controls the arc of the projectile.
*   **`speed_min="100"`, `speed_max="220"`**: Defines the projectile's speed range.
*   **`die_on_low_velocity="0"`**: The projectile does not disappear if its speed drops too low.
*   **`on_death_explode="1"`**: The projectile explodes upon death.
*   **`on_lifetime_out_explode="1"`**: The projectile explodes when its lifetime expires.
*   **`explosion_dont_damage_shooter="1"`**: The explosion does not harm the entity that fired it.
*   **`damage="0.3"`**: Base damage of the projectile.
*   **`knockback_force="2.0"`**: The force applied to knock back entities upon collision.
*   **`bounces_left="10"`**: The projectile can bounce up to 10 times.
*   **`bounce_always="1"`**: The projectile will always attempt to bounce.
*   **`lifetime="100"`**: The projectile exists for 100 frames before expiring.

### Explosion Configuration (`config_explosion`)

*   **`camera_shake="0.5"`**: The explosion causes moderate camera shake.
*   **`explosion_radius="1"`**: The radius of the explosion effect.
*   **`explosion_sprite="data/particles/explosion_016_plasma_pink.xml"`**: The visual sprite used for the explosion.
*   **`create_cell_material="plasma_fading"`**: Creates fading plasma cells upon explosion.
*   **`ray_energy="10000"`**: High energy for potential ray interactions.
*   **`hole_destroy_liquid="1"`**: The explosion can destroy liquids.
*   **`hole_enabled="1"`**: Creates a hole in surfaces upon explosion.
*   **`damage="0.1"`**: Damage dealt by the explosion itself.
*   **`sparks_enabled="1"`**: Sparks are generated by the explosion.
*   **`spark_material="plasma_fading_pink"`**: The material of the sparks.
*   **`light_r="155"`, `light_g="15"`, `light_b="140"`**: Defines the color of the light emitted by the explosion.

### Visuals (`SpriteComponent`)

*   **`image_file="data/projectiles_gfx/orb_dark.xml"`**: Specifies the graphical asset for the projectile.
*   **`emissive="1"`, `additive="1"`**: The sprite is emissive and uses additive blending for a glowing effect.

### Lighting (`LightComponent`)

*   **`radius="150"`**: The radius of the light emitted by the projectile.
*   **`r="250"`, `g="35"`, `b="220"`**: Defines the bright pinkish-purple color of the light.

### Particle Emitter (`SpriteParticleEmitterComponent`)

*   **`sprite_file="data/particles/spark_electric.xml"`**: Uses electric sparks as particles.
*   **`color.r="1" color.g="0" color.b="1" color.a="1"`**: The initial color of the sparks is purple.
*   **`gravity.y="10"`**: Sparks are affected by gravity.
*   **`emission_interval_min_frames="3"`, `emission_interval_max_frames="4"`**: Controls the frequency of particle emission.
*   **`count_min="1" count_max="2"`**: Emits 1 to 2 particles per emission.
*   **`randomize_position`**: Particles are emitted with slight random offsets.

### Lua Scripting (`LuaComponent`)

*   **`script_source_file="data/entities/animals/boss_alchemist/init.lua"`**: Executes initialization logic when the entity is added.
*   **`script_source_file="data/entities/animals/boss_alchemist/create_wand.lua"`**: Executes a script every 32 frames, likely related to wand creation or behavior.

### Audio (`AudioComponent`, `AudioLoopComponent`)

*   **`file="data/audio/Desktop/projectiles.bank"`**: Specifies the audio bank.
*   **`event_root="projectiles/orb_dark"`**: The root event for the projectile's sounds.
*   **`event_name="projectiles/magic_orb/loop"`**: A looping sound effect for the projectile.

### Variables (`VariableStorageComponent`)

*   **`name="projectile_file"`, `value_string="data/entities/animals/boss_alchemist/wand_orb.xml"`**: Stores the entity's own file path, useful for referencing.
