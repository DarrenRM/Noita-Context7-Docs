# Glitter Bomb Projectile

This document describes the `glitter_bomb.xml` entity, which defines the behavior and appearance of the Glitter Bomb projectile in Noita.

## Core Attributes

The Glitter Bomb is a player projectile with a distinct visual and explosive effect.

*   **`name`**: `$projectile_default` (Inherited from base projectile)
*   **`tags`**: `projectile_player` (Identifies it as a player-created projectile)

## Physics and Appearance

### `Base file="data/entities/base_projectile_physics.xml"`

This section inherits fundamental physics properties from the base projectile.

*   **`PhysicsImageShapeComponent`**:
    *   `image_file`: `data/projectiles_gfx/glitter_bomb.png` - Specifies the visual sprite for the projectile.
    *   `material`: `fuse_tnt` - Defines the physical material properties, influencing interactions and durability.
*   **`PhysicsThrowableComponent`**:
    *   `throw_force_coeff`: `2.0` - Affects how strongly the projectile is thrown.

### `ProjectileComponent`

This component governs the projectile's flight, damage, and explosion behavior.

*   **`speed_min` / `speed_max`**: `800` - The projectile travels at a constant speed.
*   **`damage`**: `0` - The projectile itself deals no direct damage.
*   **`lifetime`**: `50` - The projectile exists for 50 frames before expiring.
*   **`on_death_explode`**: `1` - Triggers an explosion when the projectile is destroyed by other means.
*   **`on_lifetime_out_explode`**: `1` - Triggers an explosion when its lifetime expires.

#### `config_explosion`

Details of the explosion triggered by the Glitter Bomb.

*   **`damage`**: `1` - The explosion deals minimal direct damage.
*   **`explosion_radius`**: `16` - The area of effect for the explosion.
*   **`max_durability_to_destroy`**: `11` - The projectile can destroy objects with up to this durability (matches `fuse_tnt`).
*   **`camera_shake`**: `10` - The intensity of camera shake during the explosion.
*   **`ray_energy`**: `4000000` - The energy value associated with the explosion's magical effects.
*   **`stains_enabled`**: `1` - Enables stain decals on surfaces hit by the explosion.
*   **`stains_radius`**: `16` - The radius for stain decals.
*   **`explosion_sprite`**: `data/particles/explosion_016.xml` - The visual effect for the explosion.
*   **`load_this_entity`**: `data/entities/projectiles/deck/glitter_bomb_explosion.xml` - The entity spawned upon explosion, likely containing the glitter effect.
*   **`spark_material`**: `spark_purple_bright` - The material used for sparks generated by the explosion.
*   **`audio_event_name`**: `explosions/glitter` - The sound event triggered by the explosion.

## Visual Effects (Particle Emitters)

The Glitter Bomb utilizes several `ParticleEmitterComponent`s to create its visual flair.

### Smoke Emitter

*   **`emitted_material_name`**: `smoke`
*   **`count_min` / `count_max`**: `0` to `4`
*   **`lifetime_min` / `lifetime_max`**: `0.1` to `0.3`
*   **`create_real_particles`**: `1` (Emits actual particles)
*   **`emit_cosmetic_particles`**: `0` (Does not emit cosmetic particles)

### Cosmetic Spark Emitter (Primary)

*   **`emitted_material_name`**: `spark`
*   **`count_min` / `count_max`**: `1` to `2`
*   **`lifetime_min` / `lifetime_max`**: `0.1` to `0.3`
*   **`create_real_particles`**: `0` (Emits cosmetic particles)
*   **`emit_cosmetic_particles`**: `1` (Emits cosmetic particles)

### Sparse Glitter Trail Emitter

*   **`emitted_material_name`**: `spark`
*   **`count_min` / `count_max`**: `1` to `1`
*   **`lifetime_min` / `lifetime_max`**: `1.4` to `1.5`
