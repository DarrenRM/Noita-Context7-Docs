# Orbit Fireball Projectile

This document describes the `orbit_fireballs_fireball.xml` entity, which defines a projectile used in the orbiting fireball mechanic.

## Core Components

### Base Projectile (`BaseComponent`)

This component inherits from the default projectile behavior.

*   **`gravity_y`**: `0` - The fireball experiences no vertical gravity.
*   **`mass`**: `0.09` - A small mass value.

### Projectile Behavior (`ProjectileComponent`)

This is the primary component defining the projectile's actions and properties.

*   **`lob_min` / `lob_max`**: `0.8` / `1.0` - Controls the lobbing behavior, suggesting a relatively straight trajectory.
*   **`speed_min` / `speed_max`**: `0` / `0` - The projectile starts with zero initial velocity, likely meaning it's spawned with a specific velocity from its origin.
*   **`die_on_low_velocity`**: `0` - The projectile does not die if its velocity becomes low.
*   **`on_death_explode`**: `1` - The projectile explodes upon death.
*   **`on_lifetime_out_explode`**: `1` - The projectile explodes when its lifetime expires.
*   **`die_on_liquid_collision`**: `1` - The projectile is destroyed upon colliding with liquids.
*   **`damage`**: `0` - The projectile itself deals no direct damage. Damage is handled by its explosion.
*   **`on_collision_die`**: `1` - The projectile dies on collision with anything.
*   **`lifetime`**: `7200` - A very long lifetime, indicating it's meant to persist for a significant duration.
*   **`shoot_light_flash_radius`**: `150` - The radius of the light flash when shot.
*   **`knockback_force`**: `1.5` - The force applied to knock back entities on explosion.
*   **`physics_impulse_coeff`**: `2800` - Coefficient for physics impulse on collision/explosion.

#### Damage by Type

*   **`fire`**: `0.16` - Deals a small amount of fire damage.

#### Explosion Configuration (`config_explosion`)

Defines the properties of the explosion when the projectile dies.

*   **`camera_shake`**: `7.5` - The intensity of camera shake upon explosion.
*   **`explosion_radius`**: `15` - The radius of the explosion effect.
*   **`explosion_sprite`**: `data/particles/explosion_040_poof.xml` - The visual effect for the explosion.
*   **`create_cell_probability`**: `100` - Always creates material cells upon explosion.
*   **`create_cell_material`**: `fire` - The material created is fire.
*   **`hole_enabled`**: `1` - Creates a hole in the terrain.
*   **`damage`**: `0.8` - The damage dealt by the explosion.
*   **`ray_energy`**: `50000` - High energy for ray interactions.
*   **`physics_explosion_power.min` / `max`**: `0.6` / `0.9` - The minimum and maximum power of the physics-based explosion.
*   **`shake_vegetation`**: `1` - Shakes vegetation upon explosion.
*   **`sparks_enabled`**: `1` - Sparks are generated by the explosion.
*   **`stains_enabled`**: `1` - Creates stains on surfaces from the explosion.
*   **`audio_enabled`**: `1` - Plays an explosion sound.

### Visuals (`SpriteComponent`)

Defines the visual appearance of the projectile.

*   **`image_file`**: `data/projectiles_gfx/grenade_large.xml` - Uses a large grenade graphic.
*   **`additive`**: `1` - The sprite uses additive blending, making it appear brighter.

### Particle Emitters

This entity utilizes multiple `ParticleEmitterComponent`s to create visual effects.

#### Smoke Emitter

*   **`emitted_material_name`**: `smoke` - Emits smoke particles.
*   **`count_min` / `max`**: `5` / `5` - Emits a fixed number of particles.
*   **`lifetime_min` / `max`**: `0.1` / `0.3` - Short particle lifetime.
*   **`create_real_particles`**: `1` - Creates actual game particles.

#### Spark Trail Emitter

*   **`emitted_material_name`**: `spark` - Emits spark particles.
*   **`gravity.y`**: `-100.60` - Sparks have upward gravity.
*   **`is_trail`**: `1` - Creates a trail effect.
*   **`render_on_grid`**: `1` - Renders sparks on the game grid.
*   **`fade_based_on_lifetime`**: `1` - Sparks fade as their lifetime decreases.
*   **`emit_cosmetic_particles`**: `1` - Emits cosmetic particles (visual only).

#### Fire Emitter

*   **`emitted_material_name`**: `fire` - Emits fire particles.
*   **`count_min` / `max`**: `1` / `1` - Emits a single fire particle.
*   **`lifetime_min` / `max`**: `0.1` / `0.3` - Short particle lifetime.
*   **`create_real_particles`**: `1` - Creates actual game particles.

### Material Conversion (`MagicConvertMaterialComponent`)

This component allows the projectile to convert materials it touches.

*   **`ignite_materials`**: `99` - A high value indicating it can ignite many materials.
*   **`radius`**: `15` - The radius of the conversion effect.

### Variable Storage (`VariableStorageComponent`)

*   **`name`**: `projectile_file`
*   **`value_string`**: `data/entities/projectiles/deck/fireball.xml` - Stores a reference to the actual fireball projectile definition.
