# Ghostly Fireball Projectile

This document details the configuration of the "Ghostly Fireball" projectile entity in Noita, focusing on its key attributes for AI-assisted modding.

## Core Attributes

The `Entity` definition for the Ghostly Fireball primarily relies on inheriting from `base_projectile.xml` and then overriding or adding specific components.

### `Base` Component

This component inherits fundamental projectile properties.

*   **`VelocityComponent`**:
    *   `gravity_y`: `100` - Controls the downward acceleration due to gravity.
    *   `mass`: `0.09` - Affects how the projectile interacts with physics.

### `ProjectileComponent`

This is the main component defining the projectile's behavior and effects.

*   **`lob_min` / `lob_max`**: `0.8` / `1.0` - Determines the arc of the projectile.
*   **`speed_min` / `speed_max`**: `160` / `170` - Sets the initial velocity range.
*   **`die_on_low_velocity`**: `1` - The projectile will disappear if its speed drops too low.
*   **`on_death_explode`**: `1` - Triggers an explosion upon death.
*   **`on_lifetime_out_explode`**: `1` - Triggers an explosion when its lifetime expires.
*   **`explosion_dont_damage_shooter`**: `1` - Prevents the projectile's explosion from harming the entity that fired it.
*   **`die_on_liquid_collision`**: `1` - The projectile is destroyed upon contact with liquids.
*   **`damage`**: `0` - Base damage of the projectile itself (before explosion).
*   **`on_collision_die`**: `1` - The projectile dies on any collision.
*   **`lifetime`**: `60` - The maximum duration in frames before the projectile expires.
*   **`muzzle_flash_file`**: `data/entities/particles/muzzle_flashes/muzzle_flash_magic_launcher_large_blue.xml` - Specifies the visual effect at the muzzle when fired.
*   **`shoot_light_flash_r` / `g` / `b` / `radius`**: `15` / `140` / `255` / `150` - Defines the color and radius of the light flash emitted when the projectile is fired.
*   **`knockback_force`**: `1.5` - The force applied to entities hit by the projectile.

#### `damage_by_type`

*   `fire`: `0.25` - The projectile deals 0.25 damage of type "fire".

#### `config_explosion`

This nested component defines the properties of the explosion that occurs on death.

*   **`never_cache`**: `1` - Prevents the explosion from being cached, ensuring it's always generated fresh.
*   **`camera_shake`**: `7.5` - The intensity of camera shake caused by the explosion.
*   **`explosion_radius`**: `15` - The area of effect for the explosion.
*   **`explosion_sprite`**: `data/particles/explosion_040_poof_blue.xml` - The visual sprite for the explosion.
*   **`create_cell_probability`**: `100` - The chance (100%) that the explosion will create material cells.
*   **`create_cell_material`**: `fire_blue` - The material of the cells created by the explosion.
*   **`hole_enabled`**: `1` - Enables the creation of holes in the environment.
*   **`damage`**: `2` - The damage dealt by the explosion itself.
*   **`ray_energy`**: `50000` - The energy of the explosion's rays.
*   **`hole_image`**: `data/temp/explosion_hole.png` - The image used for the explosion hole.
*   **`particle_effect`**: `1` - Enables particle effects for the explosion.
*   **`damage_mortals`**: `1` - The explosion damages living entities.
*   **`physics_explosion_power.min` / `max`**: `0.6` / `0.9` - The minimum and maximum force of the physics-based explosion.
*   **`physics_throw_enabled`**: `1` - Enables physics-based throwing of objects by the explosion.
*   **`shake_vegetation`**: `1` - The explosion shakes vegetation.
*   **`sparks_enabled`**: `1` - Enables sparks to be generated by the explosion.
*   **`spark_material`**: `spark_blue` - The material of the sparks.
*   **`sparks_count_min` / `max`**: `50` / `100` - The range for the number of sparks.
*   **`light_fade_time`**: `0.8` - How long the explosion's light lasts.
*   **`stains_enabled`**: `1` - Enables stains to be left by the explosion.
*   **`stains_image`**: `data/temp/explosion_stain.png` - The image used for explosion stains.
*   **`audio_event_name`**: `explosions/fireball` - The sound event triggered by the explosion.

### `SpriteComponent`

Defines the visual appearance of the projectile.

*   **`image_file`**: `data/projectiles_gfx/fireball_ghostly.xml` - The sprite sheet or definition for the projectile's graphics.
*   **`offset_x` / `offset_y`**: `16` / `12` - Adjusts the sprite's position relative to the entity's origin.
*   **`additive`**: `1` - Enables additive blending for the sprite, often used for glowing effects.
