# Blue Orb Projectile

This document details the configuration of the "Blue Orb" projectile entity in Noita, focusing on its key attributes for AI-assisted modding.

## Entity Definition

The core of the projectile is defined by the `<Entity>` tag, inheriting base projectile properties.

```xml
<Entity 
  name="$projectile_default" 
   >
  </Entity>
```

## Base Projectile Properties

Inherited from `base_projectile.xml`, these define fundamental physics.

### VelocityComponent

*   **gravity_y**: `0` - The projectile is not affected by gravity.
*   **air_friction**: `0` - No air resistance.
*   **mass**: `0.05` - A very small mass, contributing to its speed.

```xml
	<Base file="data/entities/base_projectile.xml" >
		<VelocityComponent
    		gravity_y="0"
			air_friction="0"
			mass="0.05"
			>
    	</VelocityComponent> 
	</Base>
```

## Projectile Component

This component governs the projectile's behavior, damage, and effects.

### ProjectileComponent

*   **lob_min/lob_max**: `0.8` / `1.0` - Controls the projectile's lobbing behavior.
*   **speed_min/speed_max**: `150` / `150` - The projectile travels at a constant speed of 150 units.
*   **die_on_low_velocity**: `0` - The projectile does not die when its velocity drops.
*   **on_death_explode**: `1` - The projectile explodes upon death.
*   **on_lifetime_out_explode**: `1` - The projectile explodes when its lifetime expires.
*   **explosion_dont_damage_shooter**: `1` - The explosion does not harm the entity that fired it.
*   **damage**: `0.8` - The base damage dealt by the projectile.
*   **on_collision_die**: `1` - The projectile dies upon collision.
*   **lifetime**: `100` - The projectile exists for 100 frames before expiring.
*   **knockback_force**: `1.3` - The force applied to knock back entities upon collision.

#### config_explosion

Defines the parameters of the explosion when the projectile dies or its lifetime expires.

*   **never_cache**: `1` - Ensures the explosion is always generated fresh.
*   **camera_shake**: `0` - No camera shake effect from the explosion.
*   **explosion_radius**: `6` - The radius of the explosion.
*   **explosion_sprite**: `data/particles/explosion_016_plasma.xml` - The visual effect for the explosion.
*   **ray_energy**: `5000` - The energy of any rays generated by the explosion.
*   **hole_destroy_liquid**: `1` - The explosion can destroy liquids.
*   **hole_enabled**: `1` - The explosion creates holes in surfaces.
*   **hole_image**: `data/temp/explosion_hole.png` - The image used for the explosion hole.
*   **physics_explosion_power.min/max**: `0.3` / `0.4` - The minimum and maximum physics force applied by the explosion.
*   **shake_vegetation**: `1` - The explosion shakes vegetation.
*   **light_r/g/b**: `10` / `60` / `10` - The RGB values for the light emitted by the explosion.

```xml
  <ProjectileComponent 
    _enabled="1" 
	lob_min="0.8"
  	lob_max="1.0"
  	speed_min="150"
  	speed_max="150"
    die_on_low_velocity="0"
    on_death_explode="1"
    on_death_gfx_leave_sprite="0" 
    on_lifetime_out_explode="1"
	explosion_dont_damage_shooter="1"
    damage="0.8"
    on_collision_die="1"
    lifetime="100" 
	knockback_force="1.3"
	>
	<config_explosion
		never_cache="1" 
		camera_shake="0" 
		explosion_radius="6" 
		explosion_sprite="data/particles/explosion_016_plasma.xml" 
		explosion_sprite_lifetime="0.0" 
		create_cell_probability="0" 
		create_cell_material="" 
		ray_energy="5000"
		hole_destroy_liquid="1" 
		hole_enabled="1" 
		hole_image="data/temp/explosion_hole.png"
		explosion_sprite_emissive="1"
		explosion_sprite_additive="1"	
		particle_effect="0" 
		damage_mortals="0" 
		physics_explosion_power.min="0.3" 
		physics_explosion_power.max="0.4" 
		physics_throw_enabled="1" 
		shake_vegetation="1" 
		sparks_enabled="0" 
		sparks_count_max="0" 
		sparks_count_min="0"
		light_fade_time="0.8" 
		light_r="10"
		light_g="60"
		light_b="10"
		stains_enabled="0" 
		stains_image="data/temp/explosion_stain.png"
		audio_enabled="0" >
	</config_explosion>
  </ProjectileComponent>
```

## Visual Components

These define the graphical representation of the projectile.

### SpriteComponent (x3)

*   **image_file**: `data/projectiles_gfx/orb_blue.xml` (for the first two) and `data/projectiles_gfx/lance.xml` (for the third). These specify the sprite sheets used.
*   **offset_x/y**: `6` / `6` for the first two, centering the sprite.
*   **rect_animation**: `spawn` for the first two, indicating an animation state.
*   **emissive/additive**: `1` for the second sprite, making it glow and blend additively. The third sprite is also additive.

```xml
  <SpriteComponent 
    _enabled="1" 
    alpha="1" 
    image_file="data/projectiles_gfx/orb_blue.xml" 
    next_rect_animation="" 
    offset_x="6" 
    offset_y="6" 
    rect_animation="spawn" 
     
     
	update_transform_rotation="0"
	>
  </SpriteComponent>
  
  <SpriteComponent 
    _enabled="1" 
    alpha="1" 
    image_file="data/projectiles_gfx/orb_blue.xml" 
    next_rect_animation="" 
    offset_x="6" 
    offset_y="6" 
    rect_animation="spawn" 
	emissive="1"
    additive="1"
     
	update_transform_rotation="0"
	>
  </SpriteComponent>
  
   <SpriteComponent 
    _enabled="1" 
    alpha="1" 
    image_file="data/projectiles_gfx/lance.xml" 
    next_rect_animation=""
    rect_animation="" 
    additive="1"
     >
  </SpriteComponent>
```

## Particle Emitter

This component generates visual particles.

### ParticleEmitterComponent

*   **emitted_material_name**: `spark_blue` - The material of the particles emitted.
*   **gravity.y**: `0.10` - The particles are affected by a slight downward gravity.
*   **count\_min/max**: `1` / `5` - The number of particles emitted per burst.
*   **lifetime\_min/max**: `0.6` / `0.8` - The duration particles exist.
*   **is\_trail**: `1` - Particles form a trail.
*   **create\_real\_particles**: `0` - These are cosmetic particles, not physical entities.
*   **is\_emitting**: `1` - The emitter is active.

```xml
	<ParticleEmitterComponent 
		emitted_material_name="spark_blue"
		gravity.y="0.10"
		offset.x="0"
		offset.y="0"
		gravity.y="0.10"
		x_pos_offset_min="-1"
		y_pos_offset_min="-1"
		x_pos_offset_max="1"
		y_pos_offset_max="1"
		x_vel_min="0"
		x_vel_max="0"
		y_vel_min="0"
		y_vel_max="0"
		count_min="1"
		count_max="5"
		lifetime_min="0.6"
		lifetime_max="0.8"
		render_on_grid="1"
		is_trail="1"
		trail_gap="1"
		airflow_force="0.1"
		airflow_time="0.101"
		airflow_scale="2.01"
		fade_based_on_lifetime="1"
		create_real_particles="0"
		emit_cosmetic_particles="1"
		emission_interval_min_frames="0"
		emission_interval_max_frames="1"
		is_emitting="1" >
    </ParticleEmitterComponent>
```

## Light Component

Adds a light source to the projectile.

### LightComponent

*   **radius**: `150` - The radius of the light.
*   **r/g/b**: `30` / `30` / `90` - The blueish color of the light.

```xml
  <LightComponent 
    _enabled="1" 
    radius="150" 
    r="30"
    g="30"
    b="90">
  </LightComponent>
```

## Lua Component

Attaches custom Lua scripting to the projectile.

### LuaComponent

*   **script\_source\_file**: `data/scripts/projectiles/orb_blue.lua` - The path to the associated Lua script.
*   **execute\_every\_n\_frame**: `5` - The script executes every 5 frames.

```xml
  <LuaComponent 
		_enabled="1" 
		script_source_file="data/scripts/projectiles/orb_blue.lua" 
		execute_every_n_frame="5">
   </LuaComponent>
```

## Audio Component

Defines the sound effects for the projectile.

### AudioComponent

*   **file**: `data/audio/Desktop/projectiles.bank` - The audio bank file.
*   **event\_root**: `projectiles/orb_b` - The root event name for projectile sounds.

```xml
	<AudioComponent
		file="data/audio/Desktop/projectiles.bank"
		event_root="projectiles/orb_b" >
	</AudioComponent>
```

## Variable Storage Component

Stores custom variables associated with the entity.

### VariableStorageComponent

*   **name**: `projectile_file` - The name of the variable.
*   **value\_string**: `data/entities/projectiles/orb_blue.xml` - The string value, referencing its own file.

```xml
	<VariableStorageComponent
		name="projectile_file"
		value_string="data/entities/projectiles/orb_blue.xml"
		>
	</VariableStorageComponent>
```
