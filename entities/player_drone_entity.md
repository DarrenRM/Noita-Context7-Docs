# Player Drone Entity

This document details the configuration of the Player Drone entity in Noita, primarily focusing on its AI, abilities, and physical attributes.

## Core Components

The Player Drone is a unique entity that functions similarly to a player-controlled projectile or companion. It inherits many properties from `base_enemy_robot.xml`.

### AbilityComponent

This component defines the drone's primary function as a deployable entity with a limited inventory and specific firing mechanics.
