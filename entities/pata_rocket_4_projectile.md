## Particle Emitters

This projectile utilizes multiple `ParticleEmitterComponent`s to create visual effects.

### Emitter 1 (Smoke Trail)

*   **`emitted_material_name`**: `smoke`
*   **`count_min`/`max`**: `5`/`5`
*   **`lifetime_min`/`max`**: `0.1`/`0.3`
*   **`is_emitting`**: `1`

### Emitters 2-5 (Plasma Trail)

These emitters generate `plasma_fading_pink` particles, creating a trail effect. They vary in:

*   **`gravity.y`**: Some have positive gravity, some negative, and one is zero, creating varied trail behavior.
*   **`lifetime_min`/`max`**: Ranges from `0.3` to `1.6` seconds.
*   **`airflow_force`/`time`/`scale`**: These parameters influence how particles react to air currents.
*   **`is_trail`**: `1` for trail effects.
*   **`fade_based_on_lifetime`**: Controls how particles fade out.

### Emitter 6 (Sparse Trail)

*   **`emitted_material_name`**: `plasma_fading_pink`
*   **`emission_interval_min_frames`/`max_frames`**: `8`/`12` (Emits particles less frequently).
*   **`lifetime_min`/`max`**: `1.4`/`2.5`
*   **`is_trail`**: `0` (Not a continuous trail, but individual particles).

### Emitter 7 (Explosion Sparks/Debris)
