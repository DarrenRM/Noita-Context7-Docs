# Machinegun Bullet Projectile

This document details the configuration for the `machinegun_bullet.xml` entity, representing a standard bullet projectile fired from a machine gun.

## Core Components

### Base Projectile (`Base file="data/entities/base_projectile.xml"`)

This section inherits fundamental projectile properties.

*   **`VelocityComponent`**:
    *   `mass`: `0.05` - Defines the projectile's mass.

### Projectile Configuration (`ProjectileComponent`)

This is the primary component defining the bullet's behavior and characteristics.

*   **`_enabled`**: `1` - Enables this component.
*   **`lob_min` / `lob_max`**: `0.8` / `0.9` - Controls the minimum and maximum "lob" or arc of the projectile.
*   **`speed_min` / `speed_max`**: `400` / `500` - Sets the initial speed range of the projectile.
*   **`friction`**: `1.3` - Determines how quickly the projectile loses speed due to air resistance.
*   **`direction_random_rad`**: `0.1` - Introduces a small amount of randomness to the projectile's initial direction.
*   **`on_death_explode`**: `1` - Causes the projectile to explode upon death (e.g., hitting a surface or expiring).
*   **`on_lifetime_out_explode`**: `1` - Triggers an explosion when the projectile's lifetime expires.
*   **`explosion_dont_damage_shooter`**: `1` - Prevents the projectile's explosion from damaging the entity that fired it.
*   **`on_collision_die`**: `1` - The projectile dies upon colliding with an entity.
*   **`lifetime`**: `50` - The duration in frames before the projectile expires.
*   **`knockback_force`**: `0.4` - The force applied to entities hit by the projectile, causing them to be knocked back.
*   **`camera_shake_when_shot`**: `2.0` - The intensity of camera shake experienced by the shooter when this projectile is fired.
*   **`shoot_light_flash_radius`**: `64` - The radius of the light flash effect emitted when the projectile is shot.
*   **`create_shell_casing`**: `1` - Enables the creation of a shell casing effect when the projectile is fired.
*   **`shell_casing_offset.x` / `.y`**: `-10` / `-4` - The offset position for the shell casing relative to the firing point.
*   **`bounces_left`**: `0` - The number of times the projectile can bounce off surfaces.
*   **`damage`**: `0.15` - The base damage dealt by the projectile.
*   **`go_through_this_material`**: `aluminium_robot` - Specifies a material that the projectile can pass through without dying.
*   **`muzzle_flash_file`**: `data/entities/particles/muzzle_flashes/muzzle_flash_medium.xml` - The particle effect file used for the muzzle flash.

#### Explosion Configuration (`config_explosion`)

Defines the properties of the explosion that occurs when the projectile dies.

*   **`damage`**: `0.4` - The damage dealt by the explosion.
*   **`camera_shake`**: `0.5` - The intensity of camera shake caused by the explosion.
*   **`explosion_radius`**: `2` - The radius of the explosion effect.
*   **`explosion_sprite`**: `data/particles/explosion_008.xml` - The sprite file used for the explosion visual.
*   **`hole_enabled`**: `1` - Enables the creation of holes in surfaces by the explosion.
*   **`hole_image`**: `data/temp/explosion_hole.png` - The image used for the explosion hole.
*   **`ray_energy`**: `400000` - The energy of the explosion's destructive rays.
*   **`max_durability_to_destroy`**: `9` - The maximum durability of objects that can be destroyed by the explosion.
*   **`physics_explosion_power.min` / `.max`**: `0.08` / `0.1` - The minimum and maximum power of the physics-based explosion force.
*   **`physics_throw_enabled`**: `1` - Enables physics-based throwing of objects by the explosion.
*   **`shake_vegetation`**: `1` - Causes vegetation to shake when the explosion occurs.
*   **`sparks_count_max` / `min`**: `20` / `7` - The range for the number of sparks generated by the explosion.
*   **`stains_enabled`**: `1` - Enables the creation of impact stains from the explosion.
*   **`stains_radius`**: `3` - The radius of the impact stains.

### Sprite Configuration (`SpriteComponent`)

Defines the visual representation of the projectile.

*   **`_enabled`**: `1` - Enables this component.
*   **`image_file`**: `data/projectiles_gfx/bullet.xml` - The file containing the projectile's sprite data.

### Audio Configuration (`AudioComponent`)

Handles the sound effects associated with the projectile.

*   **`file`**: `data/audio/Desktop/projectiles.bank` - The audio bank file.
*   **`event_root`**: `projectiles/minigun` - The root event for projectile sounds.

### Variable Storage (`VariableStorageComponent`)

Stores custom variables for the entity.

*   **`name`**: `projectile_file`
*   **`value_string`**: `data/entities/projectiles/machinegun_bullet_tank.xml` - This indicates that when this projectile dies, it might spawn another projectile of type `machinegun_bullet_tank.xml`.
