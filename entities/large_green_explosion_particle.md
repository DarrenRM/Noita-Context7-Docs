# Large Green Explosion Particle

This entity defines a large green explosion effect, primarily composed of various particle effects. It acts as a container for other particle entities to create a complex visual explosion.

## Key Components

The `Entity` primarily uses `LoadEntitiesComponent` to spawn other pre-defined particle entities.
