# Long Distance Cast Projectile

This document details the configuration of the "Long Distance Cast" projectile entity in Noita, primarily focusing on its projectile behavior and explosion properties.

## Entity Definition

The core entity is defined as a player projectile with specific tags.

```xml
<Entity name="$projectile_default" tags="projectile_player" >
```

## Base Projectile Configuration

Inherits base properties from `base_projectile.xml`.

```xml
	<Base file="data/entities/base_projectile.xml" >
		<VelocityComponent
			mass="0.1"
			>
		</VelocityComponent>
	</Base>
```

### Key Attributes:

*   **mass**: `0.1` - Defines the projectile's mass.

## Projectile Component

This component governs the projectile's movement, collision, and death behaviors.

```xml
  <ProjectileComponent
    _enabled="1"
    lob_min="0.8"
    lob_max="1.0"
    speed_min="1800"
    speed_max="1800"
    friction="0.0"
    direction_random_rad="0"
    on_death_explode="1"
    on_death_gfx_leave_sprite="0"
    on_lifetime_out_explode="1"
    ground_collision_fx="1"
    on_collision_die="0"
    lifetime="4"
    damage="0"
    velocity_sets_scale="0"
    lifetime_randomness="0"
    ragdoll_force_multiplier="0.01"
    hit_particle_force_multiplier="0.01"
	penetrate_entities="1"
	penetrate_world="1"
    >
```

### Key Attributes:

*   **lob\_min/lob\_max**: `0.8` / `1.0` - Controls the projectile's lobbing behavior, influencing its arc.
*   **speed\_min/speed\_max**: `1800` - Sets a fixed high speed for the projectile.
*   **friction**: `0.0` - No friction, meaning it maintains its speed without slowing down due to air resistance.
*   **on\_death\_explode**: `1` - The projectile will explode upon death.
*   **on\_lifetime\_out\_explode**: `1` - The projectile will explode when its lifetime expires.
*   **ground\_collision\_fx**: `1` - Triggers particle effects upon colliding with the ground.
*   **on\_collision\_die**: `0` - The projectile does not die upon hitting an entity.
*   **lifetime**: `4` - The projectile exists for 4 seconds before expiring.
*   **damage**: `0` - The projectile itself deals no damage.
*   **penetrate\_entities**: `1` - Can pass through entities.
*   **penetrate\_world**: `1` - Can pass through the world geometry.

### Explosion Configuration (`config_explosion`)

Defines the properties of the explosion that occurs upon the projectile's death or lifetime expiration.

```xml
	<config_explosion
      never_cache="1"
      damage="0.0"
      camera_shake="0.5"
      explosion_radius="2"
      explosion_sprite="data/particles/explosion_008_pink.xml"
      explosion_sprite_lifetime="0"
      create_cell_probability="0"
      hole_destroy_liquid="0"
      hole_enabled="0"
      ray_energy="1000"
      max_durability_to_destroy="4"
      particle_effect="0"
      damage_mortals="0"
	  physics_explosion_power.min="0.22"
      physics_explosion_power.max="0.3"
      physics_throw_enabled="1"
      shake_vegetation="1"
      sparks_count_max="13"
      sparks_count_min="7"
      sparks_enabled="1"
	  spark_material="plasma_fading_pink"
      light_enabled="0"
      stains_enabled="0"
      stains_radius="3"
	  audio_enabled="0"
	  >
    </config_explosion>
```

#### Key Attributes:

*   **damage**: `0.0` - The explosion deals no direct damage.
*   **camera\_shake**: `0.5` - Causes moderate camera shake.
*   **explosion\_radius**: `2` - The radius of the explosion effect.
*   **explosion\_sprite**: `data/particles/explosion_008_pink.xml` - Specifies the visual sprite for the explosion.
*   **ray\_energy**: `1000` - The energy of any rays generated by the explosion.
*   **max\_durability\_to\_destroy**: `4` - The maximum durability of world objects that can be destroyed by the explosion.
*   **physics\_explosion\_power.min/max**: `0.22` / `0.3` - Defines the minimum and maximum force applied to physics objects.
*   **physics\_throw\_enabled**: `1` - Physics objects affected by the explosion will be thrown.
*   **shake\_vegetation**: `1` - Causes vegetation to shake.
*   **sparks\_count\_min/max**: `7` / `13` - The range for the number of sparks generated.
*   **spark\_material**: `plasma_fading_pink` - The material used for the generated sparks.

## Audio Component

Defines the sound event associated with this projectile.

```xml
  <AudioComponent
      file="data/audio/Desktop/projectiles.bank"
      event_root="player_projectiles/long_distance_cast">
  </AudioComponent>
```

### Key Attributes:

*   **file**: `data/audio/Desktop/projectiles.bank` - The audio bank containing the sound event.
*   **event\_root**: `player_projectiles/long_distance_cast` - The specific sound event to trigger.
