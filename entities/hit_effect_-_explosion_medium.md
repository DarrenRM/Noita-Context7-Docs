# Hit Effect - Explosion (Medium)

This entity defines a projectile that, upon death (either from low velocity, lifetime expiration, or collision), triggers a medium-sized explosion effect. It's primarily used as a visual and damaging hit effect.

## Key Components

### ProjectileComponent

This component governs the behavior of the projectile itself.
