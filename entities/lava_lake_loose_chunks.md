# Lava Lake Loose Chunks

This entity defines loose ground chunks that can appear in lava lake biomes. These chunks are designed to be destructible, allowing players to pass through them, particularly in collapsed temple areas.

## Key Components

### LooseGroundComponent

This component governs the behavior of the loose ground chunks.
