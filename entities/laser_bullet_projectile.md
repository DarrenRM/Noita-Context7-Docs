# Laser Bullet Projectile

This document details the configuration of a basic laser bullet projectile entity in Noita, primarily focusing on its physical properties, visual representation, and projectile behavior.

## Core Components

This projectile is built upon several core Noita entity components, each contributing to its overall functionality.

### Base Projectile (`Base file="data/entities/base_projectile.xml"`)

This component defines fundamental projectile physics.

*   **`VelocityComponent`**:
    *   `gravity_y`: `200` - Controls the downward acceleration due to gravity.
    *   `air_friction`: `0.3` - Resistance to movement in the air.
    *   `mass`: `0.08` - The projectile's mass, affecting its interaction with forces.

### Projectile Behavior (`ProjectileComponent`)

This is the primary component defining the projectile's unique characteristics.

*   **`_enabled`**: `1` - Enables this component.
*   **`speed_min` / `speed_max`**: `600` / `750` - The range of initial projectile speeds.
*   **`lifetime`**: `30` - The duration in frames before the projectile expires.
*   **`lifetime_randomness`**: `7` - Adds variability to the projectile's lifetime.
*   **`damage`**: `0.51` - The base damage dealt by the projectile.
*   **`on_death_explode`**: `1` - The projectile will explode upon death.
*   **`on_collision_die`**: `1` - The projectile will disappear upon colliding with something.
*   **`on_lifetime_out_explode`**: `1` - The projectile will explode when its lifetime expires.
*   **`knockback_force`**: `2.6` - The force applied to entities hit by the projectile.
*   **`camera_shake_when_shot`**: `0.1` - The amount of camera shake when this projectile is fired.
*   **`muzzle_flash_file`**: `data/entities/particles/muzzle_flashes/muzzle_flash_magic_launcher.xml` - Specifies the particle effect for the muzzle flash.
*   **`shoot_light_flash_r/g/b/radius`**: `120`/`235`/`90`/`100` - Defines the color and radius of the light flash when shot.

#### `config_explosion` (Nested within `ProjectileComponent`)

Defines the properties of the explosion that occurs when the projectile dies.

*   **`damage`**: `0.1` - Damage dealt by the explosion itself.
*   **`camera_shake`**: `2.0` - Camera shake intensity for the explosion.
*   **`explosion_radius`**: `6` - The area of effect for the explosion.
*   **`explosion_sprite`**: `data/particles/explosion_016_slime.xml` - The visual sprite for the explosion.
*   **`hole_enabled`**: `1` - Enables the creation of holes in the environment.
*   **`ray_energy`**: `300000` - The energy of the explosion's rays.
*   **`physics_explosion_power.min/max`**: `0.1` / `0.3` - The minimum and maximum force of the physics-based explosion.
*   **`stains_enabled`**: `1` - Enables environmental stains from the explosion.
*   **`stains_radius`**: `1` - The radius of the stains.
*   **`spark_material`**: `spark_green` - The material used for sparks generated by the explosion.

### Visuals (`SpriteComponent`)

Defines the projectile's appearance.

*   **`image_file`**: `data/projectiles_gfx/fireball_smaller_green.xml` - The sprite sheet or animation file.
*   **`rect_animation`**: `fireball` - The name of the animation to use from the `image_file`.
*   **`additive`**: `1` - Enables additive blending for the sprite, often used for glowing effects.

### Audio (`AudioComponent`)

Handles sound effects associated with the projectile.

*   **`file`**: `data/audio/Desktop/projectiles.bank` - The audio bank containing the sound events.
*   **`event_root`**: `player_projectiles/bullet_heavy_fast` - The specific sound event to play.

### Particle Emitters (`ParticleEmitterComponent`)

These components generate cosmetic particles to enhance the visual feedback.

#### Emitter 1 (Trail)

*   **`emitted_material_name`**: `spark_yellow` - The material of the particles emitted.
*   **`is_trail`**: `1` - Indicates this emitter creates a trail effect.
*   **`trail_gap`**: `0.6` - The spacing between trail particles.
*   **`lifetime_min/max`**: `0.1` / `0.2` - The duration of each emitted particle.
*   **`emission_interval_min_frames/max_frames`**: `1` / `2` - Controls the frequency of particle emission.

#### Emitter 2 (Impact/Motion)

*   **`emitted_material_name`**: `spark_green` - The material of the particles emitted.
*   **`is_trail`**: `1` - Indicates this emitter creates a trail effect.
*   **`trail_gap`**: `1.2` - The spacing between trail particles.
*   **`lifetime_min/max`**: `0.2` / `0.6` - The duration of each emitted particle.
*   **`x_vel_min/max`**: `20` / `80` - The horizontal velocity range of emitted particles.
*   **`emission_interval_min_frames/max_frames`**: `1` / `2` - Controls the frequency of particle emission.

### Lighting (`LightComponent`)

Adds a light source to the projectile.

*   **`radius`**: `30` - The radius of the light emitted by the projectile.
