# Orb Swapper Projectile

This document details the configuration of the Orb Swapper projectile entity in Noita, useful for AI-assisted modding.

## Base Properties

The Orb Swapper inherits from `base_projectile.xml`, defining its fundamental physics and behavior.

### Velocity Component

*   **`gravity_y`**: `0` - The projectile is not affected by gravity.
*   **`air_friction`**: `-0.1` - Minimal air resistance.
*   **`mass`**: `0.05` - A very light projectile.

### Homing Component

This component allows the projectile to track specific targets.

*   **`target_tag`**: `"prey"` - The projectile will seek entities tagged as "prey".
*   **`homing_targeting_coeff`**: `7` - A strong coefficient for homing behavior.
*   **`detect_distance`**: `350` - The maximum distance at which the projectile can detect targets.
*   **`homing_velocity_multiplier`**: `1.0` - The homing speed is directly proportional to the projectile's base speed.

### Hit Effect Component

*   **`effect_hit`**: `"SWAPPER"` - Triggers a specific "swapper" effect upon collision.

### Projectile Component

Defines the core projectile mechanics and collision behavior.

*   **`lob_min` / `lob_max`**: `0.8` / `1.0` - Controls the arc of the projectile.
*   **`speed_min` / `speed_max`**: `100` / `120` - Sets the projectile's speed range.
*   **`die_on_low_velocity`**: `0` - The projectile does not die if its velocity drops too low.
*   **`on_death_explode`**: `1` - The projectile explodes when it dies.
*   **`on_lifetime_out_explode`**: `1` - The projectile explodes when its lifetime expires.
*   **`explosion_dont_damage_shooter`**: `1` - The explosion will not harm the entity that fired it.
*   **`damage`**: `0.0` - The projectile itself deals no direct damage.
*   **`on_collision_die`**: `1` - The projectile dies upon colliding with something.
*   **`friendly_fire`**: `1` - The projectile can collide with and affect friendly entities.
*   **`lifetime`**: `150` - The projectile exists for 150 frames.

#### Config Explosion

Details the properties of the explosion triggered by the projectile.

*   **`never_cache`**: `1` - Ensures the explosion is always created fresh.
*   **`camera_shake`**: `0.5` - Causes a moderate camera shake upon explosion.
*   **`explosion_radius`**: `1` - A small explosion radius.
*   **`explosion_sprite`**: `"data/particles/explosion_016_plasma.xml"` - Uses a plasma explosion visual.
*   **`create_cell_probability`**: `1` - Always creates a cell upon explosion.
*   **`create_cell_material`**: `"plasma_fading"` - The created cell material is "plasma_fading".
*   **`ray_energy`**: `10000` - High energy for potential ray interactions.
*   **`hole_destroy_liquid`**: `1` - The explosion can destroy liquids.
*   **`hole_enabled`**: `1` - Creates a hole in terrain upon explosion.
*   **`damage`**: `0.0` - The explosion itself deals no direct damage.
*   **`shake_vegetation`**: `1` - Affects and shakes vegetation.
*   **`sparks_enabled`**: `1` - Sparks are generated by the explosion.
*   **`spark_material`**: `"plasma_fading"` - The sparks are of the "plasma_fading" material.
*   **`sparks_count_min` / `sparks_count_max`**: `12` / `16` - The number of sparks generated.
*   **`light_r` / `light_g` / `light_b`**: `35` / `135` / `140` - Defines the color of the light emitted by the explosion.
*   **`stains_enabled`**: `1` - Creates stains on surfaces from the explosion.

## Visuals and Effects

### Sprite Component

Defines the visual appearance of the projectile.

*   **`image_file`**: `"data/projectiles_gfx/orb_blue.xml"` - Uses a blue orb graphic.
*   **`rect_animation`**: `"fireball"` - Applies a "fireball" animation.
*   **`emissive`**: `1` - The sprite emits light.
*   **`additive`**: `1` - The sprite uses additive blending.

### Light Component

Adds a light source to the projectile.

*   **`radius`**: `150` - The light extends to a radius of 150 units.
*   **`r` / `g` / `b`**: `40` / `18` / `220` - The light color is a bluish-purple.

### Particle Emitter Components

Two particle emitters are used to generate visual effects.

#### Emitter 1 (Trail)

*   **`emitted_material_name`**: `"spark_blue"` - Emits blue sparks.
*   **`gravity.y`**: `-10.0` - Sparks are affected by upward gravity.
*   **`count_min` / `count_max`**: `70` / `140` - Number of particles emitted per interval.
*   **`lifetime_min` / `lifetime_max`**: `0.4` / `0.6` - Lifespan of emitted particles.
*   **`is_trail`**: `1` - Particles form a trail.
*   **`emit_cosmetic_particles`**: `1` - Emits cosmetic particles.

#### Emitter 2 (Main Effect)

*   **`emitted_material_name`**: `"spark_blue"` - Emits blue sparks.
*   **`gravity.y`**: `-5.0` - Sparks are affected by upward gravity.
*   **`count_min` / `count_max`**: `70` / `140` - Number of particles emitted per interval.
*   **`lifetime_min` / `lifetime_max`**: `0.4` / `3.6` - Lifespan of emitted particles.
*   **`airflow_force`**: `3.1` - Strong airflow influence on particles.
*   **`is_trail`**: `1` - Particles form a trail.
*   **`emit_cosmetic_particles`**: `1` - Emits cosmetic particles.
