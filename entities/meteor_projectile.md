# Meteor Projectile

This document details the configuration of the "meteor" projectile entity in Noita, focusing on its behavior, visual effects, and damage properties.

## Core Projectile Properties

The `ProjectileComponent` defines the fundamental characteristics of the meteor projectile.

### Key Attributes:

*   **`lob_min`, `lob_max`**: Controls the arc of the projectile. Values between 0.8 and 1.0 suggest a relatively straight trajectory with minimal lobbing.
*   **`speed_min`, `speed_max`**: Defines the projectile's initial velocity, ranging from 300 to 400 units.
*   **`die_on_low_velocity`**: If true, the projectile is destroyed when its speed drops below a certain threshold.
*   **`on_death_explode`**: Triggers an explosion upon the projectile's destruction.
*   **`on_lifetime_out_explode`**: Triggers an explosion when the projectile's lifetime expires.
*   **`damage`**: The base damage dealt by the projectile. Set to 0, indicating damage is primarily from its explosion.
*   **`on_collision_die`**: The projectile is destroyed upon colliding with an entity.
*   **`die_on_liquid_collision`**: The projectile is destroyed upon colliding with liquids.
*   **`lifetime`**: The duration in frames before the projectile expires (200 frames).
*   **`knockback_force`**: The force applied to entities hit by the projectile's explosion (3.0).
*   **`physics_impulse_coeff`**: Coefficient for physics impulse calculations during explosion (9000).

### Damage Types:

*   **`damage_by_type`**: Specifies damage multipliers for different damage types.
    *   `fire="2.25"`: The explosion deals 2.25 times fire damage.

### Explosion Configuration (`config_explosion`):

This section details the parameters of the explosion triggered by the meteor.

*   **`camera_shake`**: The intensity of camera shake upon explosion (20.5).
*   **`explosion_radius`**: The radius of the explosion effect (45 units).
*   **`explosion_sprite`**: The visual sprite used for the explosion effect (`data/particles/explosion_040_poof.xml`).
*   **`create_cell_probability`**: Probability of creating material cells upon explosion (100%).
*   **`create_cell_material`**: The material created by the explosion cells (`fire`).
*   **`hole_enabled`**: Whether the explosion creates holes in the terrain (enabled).
*   **`hole_image`**: The image used for the explosion hole (`data/temp/explosion_hole.png`).
*   **`ray_energy`**: The energy of the explosion's damaging rays (7500000).
*   **`damage_mortals`**: Whether the explosion damages living entities (enabled).
*   **`physics_explosion_power.min`, `physics_explosion_power.max`**: The minimum and maximum power of the physics-based explosion (2 to 4).
*   **`physics_throw_enabled`**: Whether the explosion can throw entities (enabled).
*   **`sparks_enabled`**: Whether sparks are generated by the explosion (enabled).
*   **`sparks_count_min`, `sparks_count_max`**: The range of spark counts (50 to 100).
*   **`stains_enabled`**: Whether stains are left by the explosion (enabled).
*   **`stains_image`**: The image used for explosion stains (`data/temp/explosion_stain.png`).

## Visuals

### Sprite Component:

*   **`image_file`**: The primary sprite for the meteor projectile (`data/projectiles_gfx/meteor.xml`).
*   **`emissive`, `additive`**: These flags indicate the sprite uses emissive and additive rendering for a glowing effect.
*   **`z_index`**: Controls the rendering layer of the sprite (-0.5, placing it slightly behind other elements).

### Particle Emitters:

The meteor utilizes several `ParticleEmitterComponent`s to create visual effects:

1.  **Smoke Emitter**:
    *   `emitted_material_name="smoke"`: Emits smoke particles.
    *   `count_min="5"`, `count_max="5"`: Emits a consistent number of particles.
    *   `lifetime_min="0.1"`, `lifetime_max="0.3"`: Short particle lifespan.
    *   `create_real_particles="1"`: Creates actual game particles.

2.  **Fire Emitter (Initial Burst)**:
    *   `emitted_material_name="fire"`: Emits fire particles.
    *   `count_min="1"`, `count_max="1"`: A single, strong burst of fire.
    *   `lifetime_min="0.1"`, `lifetime_max="0.3"`: Short lifespan for these particles.

3.  **Fire Emitter (Delayed Burst)**:
    *   `emitted_material_name="fire"`: Emits fire particles.
    *   `delay_frames="2"`: Starts emitting after a short delay.
    *   `is_emitting="0"`: This emitter is controlled by other logic to trigger its emission.

4.  **Spark Emitter**:
    *   `emitted_material_name="spark"`: Emits spark particles.
    *   `count_min="40"`, `count_max="100"`: Generates a significant number of sparks.
    *   `lifetime_min="0.2"`, `lifetime_max="0.4"`: Moderate lifespan for sparks.
    *   `emit_cosmetic_particles="1"`: Emits cosmetic particles, meaning they are purely visual.
    *   `airflow_force="2.5"`, `airflow_time="1.401"`: Applies airflow to the sparks.

5.  **Explosive Fire Emitter**:
    *   `emitted_material_name="fire"`: Emits fire particles.
    *   `count_min="80"`, `count_max="120"`: A large number of particles for the explosion.
    *   `lifetime_min="0.1"`, `lifetime_max="0.2"`: Very short lifespan for these explosive particles.
    *   `emit_cosmetic_particles="1"`: Cosmetic particles.
    *   `airflow_force="8.5"`, `airflow_time="1.401"`: Stronger airflow for explosive effects.

6.  **Sprite Particle Emitter**:
    *   `sprite_file="data/particles/fire_large.xml"`: Uses a specific sprite for larger fire particles.
    *   `count_min="3"`, `count_max="5"`: Emits a small number of these larger particles.
    *   `gravity.y="10"`: Applies gravity to these particles.
    *   `randomize_rotation`, `randomize_angular_velocity`, `randomize_position`, `randomize_velocity`: These attributes introduce variation in the emitted particles' properties.

## Lighting

*   **`LightComponent`**:
    *   `radius="150"`: The meteor emits a light source with a radius of 150 units.

## Audio

*   **`AudioComponent`**:
    *   `file="data/audio/Desktop/projectiles.bank"`: Specifies the audio bank.
    *   `event_root="player_projectiles/meteor"`: Sets the root event for projectile sounds.
*   **`AudioLoopComponent`**:
    *   `event_name="player_projectiles/meteor/loop"`: Defines the looping sound event for the meteor.
    *   `auto_play="1"`: The loop starts automatically.

## Internal Variables

*   **`VariableStorageComponent`**:
    *   `name="projectile_file"`: Stores the entity's own file path for potential internal referencing.
    *   `value_string="data/entities/projectiles/deck/meteor.xml"`: The value is the path to this entity's XML file.
