# All Spells Orb Projectile

This document details the configuration for the "All Spells Orb" projectile in Noita, designed for AI-assisted modding.

## Core Projectile Properties

The `ProjectileComponent` defines the fundamental behavior and characteristics of the orb.

### Key Attributes:

*   **`lob_min` / `lob_max`**: Controls the minimum and maximum lob angle, influencing the projectile's trajectory.
    *   `lob_min="0.8"`
    *   `lob_max="1.0"`
*   **`speed_min` / `speed_max`**: Sets the projectile's initial velocity.
    *   `speed_min="350"`
    *   `speed_max="350"`
*   **`die_on_low_velocity`**: Determines if the projectile explodes when its velocity drops too low.
    *   `die_on_low_velocity="0"` (Does not explode on low velocity)
*   **`on_death_explode`**: Triggers an explosion upon the projectile's death.
    *   `on_death_explode="1"` (Explodes on death)
*   **`on_lifetime_out_explode`**: Determines if the projectile explodes when its lifetime expires.
    *   `on_lifetime_out_explode="0"` (Does not explode on lifetime out)
*   **`damage`**: The base damage dealt by the projectile.
    *   `damage="4.8"`
*   **`on_collision_die`**: If the projectile should be destroyed upon collision.
    *   `on_collision_die="1"` (Dies on collision)
*   **`lifetime`**: The duration in frames before the projectile expires.
    *   `lifetime="20"`
*   **`knockback_force`**: The force applied to entities upon collision.
    *   `knockback_force="1.0"`
*   **`friendly_fire`**: Whether the projectile can damage the player who fired it.
    *   `friendly_fire="1"` (Can damage the shooter)

## Explosion Configuration

The `config_explosion` element details the effects when the orb explodes.

### Key Attributes:

*   **`never_cache`**: Prevents caching of explosion effects for this specific instance.
    *   `never_cache="1"`
*   **`camera_shake`**: Controls the intensity of camera shake upon explosion.
    *   `camera_shake="0"` (No camera shake)
*   **`explosion_radius`**: The radius of the explosion's effect.
    *   `explosion_radius="13"`
*   **`ray_energy`**: The energy value for raycasting effects from the explosion.
    *   `ray_energy="900000"`
*   **`hole_destroy_liquid`**: Whether the explosion creates holes in liquids.
    *   `hole_destroy_liquid="1"`
*   **`hole_enabled`**: Enables the creation of holes in terrain.
    *   `hole_enabled="1"`
*   **`max_durability_to_destroy`**: The maximum durability of materials that can be destroyed by the explosion.
    *   `max_durability_to_destroy="14"`
*   **`damage`**: The damage dealt by the explosion itself.
    *   `damage="4.0"`
*   **`physics_explosion_power.min` / `physics_explosion_power.max`**: The minimum and maximum power of the physics-based explosion force.
    *   `physics_explosion_power.min="0.3"`
    *   `physics_explosion_power.max="0.4"`
*   **`sparks_enabled`**: Whether sparks are generated by the explosion.
    *   `sparks_enabled="1"`
*   **`spark_material`**: The material used for the sparks.
    *   `spark_material="plasma_fading"`
*   **`light_r` / `light_g` / `light_b`**: The RGB values for the light emitted by the explosion.
    *   `light_r="15"`
    *   `light_g="15"`
    *   `light_b="40"`

## Visual and Audio Components

### Sprite Component:

*   **`image_file`**: Specifies the graphical asset for the projectile.
    *   `image_file="data/projectiles_gfx/orb_blue.xml"`
*   **`offset_x` / `offset_y`**: Adjusts the sprite's position relative to the projectile's center.
    *   `offset_x="6"`
    *   `offset_y="6"`
*   **`emissive` / `additive`**: Controls the sprite's lighting and blending properties.
    *   `emissive="1"`
    *   `additive="1"`

### Light Component:

*   **`radius`**: The radius of the light emitted by the projectile.
    *   `radius="150"`
*   **`r` / `g` / `b`**: The RGB values for the projectile's light.
    *   `r="45"`
    *   `g="45"`
    *   `b="90"`

    *   `file="data/audio/Desktop/projectiles.bank"`
